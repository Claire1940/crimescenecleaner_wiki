## The Official Soundtrack Release

The official **crime scene cleaner ost** was developed by President Studio and features a diverse array of tracks composed by Bobkovski, Matik, and Flomblom. Released on Steam in late 2024 and updated throughout 2025 and 2026, the soundtrack is divided into two distinct discs. Disc 1 focuses primarily on the gameplay loops and atmospheric exploration, while Disc 2 contains the more surreal and unsettling tracks found during the game's nightmare sequences and "Dream Box" collectibles.

The music serves a dual purpose: it masks the grisly nature of the work with catchy, rhythmic hooks while occasionally diving into deep, unsettling drones that remind the player of the danger Kovalsky faces.
