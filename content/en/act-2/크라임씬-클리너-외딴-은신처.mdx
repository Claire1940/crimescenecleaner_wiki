## Mission Overview: White Peaks Hotel

The White Peaks Hotel was marketed as a sanctuary where AI-powered staff catered to every whim. However, the reality you encounter is far more sinister. The mission begins with a briefing from Amber, who warns you that the "Remote Retreat" has become a "nightmare fuel" zone. Your primary task is to clean up the mess left by Lars’s clients and his malfunctioning robots.

| Objective Type | Description | Difficulty |

## Mastering the 크라임씬 클리너 외딴 은신처 Cleanup

To achieve a perfect score in the **크라임씬 클리너 외딴 은신처** level, you must follow a specific order of operations. The hotel is divided into several zones, including the lobby, the massage parlor, the yoga studio, and Lars's private office. Each area has unique challenges, such as mirrors that require streak-free cleaning and tight spaces where bodies have been hidden.

### 1. Initial Entry and Lobby Clearing
Upon entering the hotel, the sheer scale of the massacre becomes apparent. Use your high-pressure washer for large floor areas but switch to the sponge for delicate surfaces like the reception desk. The AI staff may be "terminated," but their parts are scattered everywhere, requiring multiple trash bag swaps.

### 2. The Massage and Yoga Zones
The "signature experiences" of White Peaks ended in tragedy. In these rooms, you will find bodies that have been "processed" by the machines.
*   **Heat Management:** Some areas may still have active heating elements. Ensure you clear the way before attempting to move heavy objects.
*   **Mirror Cleaning:** This level features many mirrors. Use the squeegee tool to ensure no "reflective" bloodstains are left behind, as these are often missed during a cursory scan.

### 3. Lars's Office and the Associates List
The climax of the **크라임씬 클리너 외딴 은신처** mission involves reaching the top floor. Lars has escaped through a window, but he left behind crucial evidence. You must locate the list of associates, which reveals a connection to Senator Hullman, a prominent figure in the game's lore.

| Tool Recommendation | Best Used For | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High-Pressure Washer** | Large pools of blood in the lobby and outdoor areas. | **Essential** |
| **Squeegee** | Mirrors in the spa and glass partitions. | **High** |
| **UV Light** | Finding hidden blood drops behind furniture. | **Critical** |
| **Ozone Machine** | Removing the smell of "cooked" remains. | **Optional** |

## Collectibles and Secret CD Locations

A major part of completing the **크라임씬 클리너 외딴 은신처** with a 100% rating is finding the hidden CDs scattered throughout the hotel. These items are often tucked away in corners or hidden behind destructible objects.

1.  **Lobby Behind the Counter:** Check the small drawer underneath the main AI terminal.
2.  **The Spa Locker Room:** One CD is hidden inside a locker that appears jammed; you may need to clear debris to open it.
3.  **Lars's Private Balcony:** Before leaving the office area, check the snow-covered ledge outside the window Lars used to escape.
4.  **The Maintenance Closet:** Located near the yoga studio, this room contains a CD sitting on top of a stack of folded towels.

## Advanced Cleaning Tips for Perfectionists

When working through the **크라임씬 클리너 외딴 은신처**, efficiency is key. The map is large, and backtracking for forgotten trash bags can waste valuable time. 

*   **Bag Management:** Always carry a spare roll of bags. The "man versus machine" battles left a significant amount of scrap metal that fills bags quickly.
*   **Clear the Path First:** Before mopping, move all large furniture and robot husks. Blood is often trapped underneath these objects, preventing a perfect score.
*   **The Senator's Secret:** Finding the evidence regarding Senator Hullman doesn't just help your score; it unlocks additional dialogue with Amber that fleshes out the dark world of [Crime Scene Cleaner on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1040200/Crime_Scene_Cleaner/).

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Use the "Deep Clean" perk if you have it unlocked. The snowy environment of the remote retreat can make some bloodstains look like shadows, and the perk highlights them more clearly.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I find the last 1% of blood in the 크라임씬 클리너 외딴 은신처?**

A: The most common missed spots in this level are on the ceilings of the massage rooms and the very edges of the mirrors in the lobby. Use your UV light and look up!

**Q: Where is the associates list located exactly?**

A: The list is on the main desk in Lars's office on the top floor. It looks like a small folder. Make sure you pick it up before you finish the cleaning, as it is required for the "Perfect Score" achievement.

**Q: Can I destroy the robots in the hotel?**

A: Most robots are already destroyed or deactivated. However, you can move their parts and throw them into the industrial trash bins. Clearing the "tin cans" is necessary to reach the bloodstains hidden beneath them.

**Q: Is there a time limit for the 외딴 은신처 mission?**

A: There is no strict time limit, but completing the mission efficiently earns you better ratings. Most experienced players finish the cleanup and evidence collection in about 40 to 45 minutes.
