## Collecting All CDs: "We Are the Sound" Guide

One of the most significant additions in Act 2 is the "We Are the Sound" achievement, which requires you to collect all CD-DVDs. This includes not only the five new missions but also the two community maps: **Alter Ego** and **Development Hell**.

### Amber Alert CD Locations
1.  **Boxing Area:** Enter the changing room on the left side of the boxing ring. The CD is resting on a bench.
2.  **Maintenance Secret:** Climb the ladder to the maintenance room. Look for a painting on the left wall; you can walk directly through it to find a secret room containing a CD.
3.  **Head Figure Puzzle:** Locate the head figure under the stairs in the maintenance room. Carry it to the changing rooms and place it on the empty pedestal next to the other head. This opens a hidden door to the third CD.
4.  **Keg Puzzle:** In the staff area, find a keg marked with a symbol. Take it down the small hidden elevator. Arrange all four symbol-marked kegs in the order found on the bar tabs to reveal the final CD.

### Secluded Retreat CD Locations
1.  **Room of Reflection:** Follow the hallway with the piano on the first floor to the end. The CD is on the floor near a radio.
2.  **Spaceship Puzzle:** Inside the mansion, there is a dedicated spaceship room. Solve the interaction puzzle there to spawn the disk.
3.  **Waterfall Secret:** Go behind the waterfall and look for a hidden vent on the right. Crawl through the path to find the CD next to a golden duck.
4.  **Monkey Puzzle:** Collect all monkey figures in the area and arrange them in the correct sequence to unlock a secret staircase on the opposite wall.

### Circle of Friends CD Locations
1.  **Garage:** Found in the small backroom of the garage area.
2.  **Golden Terrace:** Located on a table near the greenhouse containing red flowers.
3.  **Painting Puzzle:** In the mansion's green room, select the correct paintings based on the large hint painting. This opens a floor hatch leading to a sofa where the CD sits.
4.  **Vampire Room:** Locate the vampire-themed room. You must find and hit five rubber ducks hidden around the room to unlock the chained coffin.

## Community Maps: The Final Piece

To unlock "We Are the Sound" and "Perfect Crime," you must also complete the community-integrated maps. These levels are often more complex than the standard campaign missions.

### Alter Ego CD Locations
*   **Office:** Found on a ledge behind the desk in the second-floor office (requires all fuses).
*   **Prison Block:** Move the bridge in the control room, then use a ladder to reach the vent. The CD is on a silver wagon.
*   **Testing Zone B:** Break the picture next to the Zion National Park poster and hop through the hole.

### Development Hell CD Locations
*   **Programming Room:** Tucked under a chair in the office area.
*   **Hidden Basement:** In Level -1, jump on the storage shelves to break the plank blocking the door. The CD is on a bed in the secret room.
*   **CEO's Office:** Beat the high score on the arcade machine to reveal a secret room behind the bookshelf.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to complete the community maps for the crime scene cleaner act 2 trophy guide 100% completion?**

A: Yes. Achievements like "We Are the Sound" (collecting all CDs) specifically require the CDs found in both the official Act 2 missions and the community maps "Alter Ego" and "Development Hell."

**Q: How do I unlock Nightmare mode for Act 2 missions?**

A: Nightmare mode is typically unlocked after completing the mission once on standard difficulty. Completing these is required for the "Sweet Dreams" and "Sharp Dressed Man" trophies.

**Q: I missed a cassette; can I go back and get it?**

A: Yes, you can replay any mission from the computer in your apartment. Once you pick up the collectible, you can finish the mission or leave, and it will remain registered in your collection.

**Q: What is the fastest way to earn the $50,000 required for "Money, Money, Money"?**

A: Act 2 missions like "Secluded Retreat" and "Circle of Friends" have significantly higher amounts of lootable cash and expensive items. Always check safes and secret rooms mentioned in this guide to maximize your earnings per run.
