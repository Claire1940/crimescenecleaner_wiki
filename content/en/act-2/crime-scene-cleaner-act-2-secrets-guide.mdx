## Act 2 Mission Overview: Amber Alert

The "Amber Alert" mission takes you into a multi-layered environment that includes a pizza shop, a sewer system, and a hidden underground fighting and trafficking ring. This level is a massive step up in complexity, requiring you to manage verticality and multiple "zones" of cleaning.

The secrets here often play on pop culture references, most notably the "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles." Finding the sewer-dwelling "ninja" setup is a highlight of the act. Beyond the Easter eggs, players must find specific ledger items and recordings that link the local mob to higher political figures.

| Secret Location | How to Access | Reward/Content |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Sewer Sanctuary** | Locate the manhole cover near the pizza shop kitchen. | Pizza boxes, "Ninja" gear, and a Music CD. |
| **Dave's Private Office** | Hidden behind a movable shelf in the trafficking ring area. | Evidence against Senator Holden and a Golden Item. |
| **The Loser's Locker** | Use a lockpick on the locker in the secondary changing room. | Large stash of cash and a collectible figurine. |
| **The Shaker Secret** | Interact with the specific shaker at the bar. | Lore note about the "match fixer" and poisoned drinks. |

> **Warning:** Be careful when cleaning the sewer area. The lighting is poor, and it is easy to miss blood splatters on the dark brickwork. Use your UV light frequently to ensure a perfect score.

## Perfecting the Secluded Retreat

The "Secluded Retreat" mission shifts the tone to a snowy mountain getaway at the Peaks Hotel. However, this is no vacation. The hotel is "rotten to the core," featuring killer robots, high-tech security protocols, and a dark secret involving Lars and his "associates." 

In this mission, the **crime scene cleaner act 2 secrets guide** recommends focusing on the security room first. Hacking the hotel's mainframe not only opens doors but also reveals the locations of several hidden compartments where the wealthy guests hid their illicit substances and recordings.

### Key Secrets in Secluded Retreat

1.  **The Android Graveyard:** Located in the maintenance basement. You’ll find several decommissioned "killer robots" and a secret music CD hidden inside a hollowed-out chassis.
2.  **Lars's Hidden Balcony:** In Lars's office, look for a slightly ajar window. You can climb out onto a narrow ledge to find a discarded briefcase containing high-value evidence.
3.  **The "Kinky" Suite:** A hidden room accessible via a keypad (code found on a note in the kitchen). It contains the most significant bloodstains in the level and a rare collectible.

## Advanced Cleaning Techniques for Act 2

As the scenes become more gruesome, your standard mop might not cut it. By the time you reach Act 2, you should have invested in the high-pressure power washer and the advanced chemical spray.

*   **Handling Android Parts:** In Secluded Retreat, you'll deal with mechanical debris. These cannot be mopped; they must be picked up and disposed of in heavy-duty bins.
*   **The "Pizza" Smell:** In Amber Alert, the smell of pizza can mask the odor of decomposition. Use your "Sense" ability to highlight lingering odors that might affect your final rating.
*   **Body Disposal:** Always look for the most efficient path to the van. In the Peaks Hotel, using the service elevator is much faster than dragging bags through the lobby.

> **Tip:** If you find yourself stuck at 99% cleanliness, check the ceilings. In the boxing ring area of Amber Alert, blood often splatters upward during the more violent "knockouts."

## FAQ

**Q: How do I find the code for the security room in Secluded Retreat?**

A: The code isn't on a single note. You must find the "Employee of the Month" photo in the kitchen and the "Date of Establishment" plaque in the lobby. Combining the month and year typically provides the 4-digit code, though hacking the terminal is often a faster alternative if you have the skill points.

**Q: Does finding secrets in this crime scene cleaner act 2 secrets guide affect the ending?**

A: Yes. Collecting the evidence against Senator Holden in both Amber Alert and Secluded Retreat unlocks additional dialogue options in the final act and influences the "Justice" ending of the game.

**Q: I'm missing one piece of trash in Amber Alert, where could it be?**

A: Check the sewer pipes. Often, a small pizza slice or a crumpled note can roll into the darker corners of the pipes where the "Ninja Turtles" Easter egg is located.

**Q: Are the killer robots in Act 2 dangerous?**

A: No, they are environmental hazards and part of the "mess." You are cleaning up after the robots have already malfunctioned or been destroyed. However, they are heavy and require significant inventory space to move to the dumpster.
