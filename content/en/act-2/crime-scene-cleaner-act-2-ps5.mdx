## Mastering the Massive Mansion Level

The standout mission in the Act 2 update is undoubtedly the mansion. Because it is so large, many players find themselves stuck or missing that final 1% of cleanliness. To conquer this level in **crime scene cleaner act 2 ps5**, follow these steps:

1.  **Divide and Conquer:** Treat each wing of the mansion as a separate mission. Do not move to the gardens until the interior suites are 100% clean.
2.  **Dispose of Bodies First:** Clear the "heavy lifting" early. Moving bodies to the truck first prevents you from accidentally tracking blood back into areas you've already scrubbed.
3.  **Check the Ceilings:** Some of the more violent encounters in Act 2 result in blood splatter reaching the chandeliers and high ceilings.
4.  **Key Hunt:** Keep an eye out for small silver keys on nightstands or in desk drawers. These are essential for accessing the basement and attic areas.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when smashing crates or furniture in the mansion. While it helps clear trash, creating too much debris can make it harder to spot small pieces of evidence required for mission completion.
