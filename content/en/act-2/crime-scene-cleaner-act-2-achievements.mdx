## Step-by-Step Achievement Guide

### Call Me Maybe (Missable)
As soon as the mission begins, you will find yourself in an elevator. Big Jim will call you on the radio. **Do not pick it up.** Stand still and ignore the ringing for approximately 60 seconds. Big Jim will grow increasingly frustrated, eventually threatening you. Once the achievement pops, you can safely interact with the radio to proceed.

### 100% Perfection: Evidence Locations
To earn the "Trial By Blood" perfection achievement, you must locate all eight pieces of evidence. Missing even one will result in a lower "Deception" score, preventing the 100% unlock.

| Evidence Item | Location Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Handgun** | Found inside Locker **M025** in the Men's Locker Room. |
| **Phone Recording 1** | Located on a chair in the Relaxation Room area. |
| **Ice Pick** | Found on the floor near the body by the pool. |
| **Pizza Cutter** | Located in the room with the "meat" (barbecue area). |
| **Mario Moretti Info** | Found in the locker room area after using the Move 5 keycard. |
| **Smartphone** | Found in the room to the left of the main pool area. |
| **Water Pistol** | Submerged at the bottom of the pool (drain the water first). |
| **Phone Recording 2** | Found inside Locker **Mo 17** in the Men's Locker Room. |

### Gold On The Ceiling (Secret Area)
This is one of the more involved **crime scene cleaner act 2 achievements**. Follow these specific steps:
1.  **Find the VIP Card:** Go to the Women's Locker Room and locate the **W024** keycard. Use it to find the **Mo 34** card in the Men's Lockers. Inside Mo 34, you will find the VIP Card.
2.  **The Hacking Game:** Enter the maintenance room and complete the hacking mini-game. You must hit the numbers (e.g., 3, 7, 4, 1) as the slider passes over them before your "health" bar depletes.
3.  **Drain the Pool:** Inside the secret room, interact with the fuse box and the wheel valve. This drains the bloody water from the pool.
4.  **Use the VIP Card:** Interact with the VIP reader in the secret room to unlock the achievement and open the VIP Zone door near the pool.

## Cleaning and Trash Management

Achieving 100% cleaning requires a methodical approach. The Wellness Center is divided into several zones: the Locker Rooms, the Relaxation Area, the Pool, and the VIP Zone. 

*   **The Pressure Washer:** Use this for high walls and the pillars surrounding the pool. It is less efficient for floors but vital for "hard-to-reach" spots.
*   **The UV Lamp:** In the barbecue room, the blood is baked into the surfaces. You must use the UV lamp to highlight the stains; otherwise, the game may not register them as "cleaned" even if you scrub the area.
*   **Supermassive Black Hole:** If you haven't already, Act 2 is the best place to get the achievement for picking up 100 pieces of trash. Collect every cigarette butt, broken glass shard, and discarded cup. Use your "Cleaner Sense" (Y button/Middle Mouse) to highlight trash in green.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when moving furniture like the large pots or tables. If they break, they create more trash that must be collected to maintain your 100% score.

## Cassette Tape Locations

For the "Juke Box Hero" achievement, you need to collect all music cassettes. There are three hidden in Act 2:
1.  **Locker Cassette:** Found in a locker requiring a keycard found near the pool area.
2.  **Maintenance Cassette:** Located on the desk in the room where you find the water controls (the hacking room).
3.  **VIP Cassette:** Located in the VIP Room, sitting next to the large lion statue.

## Keycard Progression Path

Keycards in Act 2 act as a puzzle. You often find a card for a locker that contains another card for a different locker.

1.  **Women's Keycard:** Found in the open locker in the Men's room.
2.  **M025:** Found in the Women's locker room; opens the handgun locker.
3.  **Move 5:** Found in the room with the pizza cutter body.
4.  **W024:** Found in the Relaxation room; opens a locker in the Women's room.
5.  **Mo 34:** Found inside the W024 locker; contains the VIP Card.
6.  **VIP Card:** Used in the secret maintenance room.
7.  **Mo 17:** Found at the bottom of the pool after draining it.
8.  **Mo 14:** Found in the final room with the two bodies.

## FAQ

**Q: Why am I stuck at 99% blood cleaned in Act 2?**

A: Check the pillars around the pool and the light fixtures on the walls. Small specks of blood often hide on the top edges of the decorative pillars. Use your UV lamp and Cleaner Sense to spot them.

**Q: How do I drain the pool for the crime scene cleaner act 2 achievements?**

A: You must enter the secret maintenance room by completing the hacking mini-game on the keypad. Once inside, turn the fuse box green and rotate the wheel valve to empty the pool.

**Q: What happens if I pick up Big Jim's radio immediately?**

A: The mission proceeds normally, but you will miss the "Call Me Maybe" achievement for that run. You would need to restart the mission to obtain it.

**Q: Do I need to flush the toilets for 100%?**

A: While not strictly required for the "Trial By Blood" mission score, there is a global achievement called "Down by the River" that requires you to flush one toilet in every mission. It is best practice to flush both the Men's and Women's toilets while you are there.
