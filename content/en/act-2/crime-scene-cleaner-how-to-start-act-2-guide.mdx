## Requirements to Unlock Act 2

Before you can access the more complex maps like the Peaks Hotel or the Sewer systems, you must satisfy specific progression milestones in Act 1. The game follows a linear structure where your home base—the apartment—serves as the hub for story progression. Act 2 officially kicks off after the completion of the "Toxic Love" mission and the subsequent interactions with Amber.

| Requirement | Description | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Complete Act 1 Missions** | Finish all primary cleanup jobs including "Bad Call" and "Toxic Love." | Mandatory |
| **Check the Computer** | Interact with the PC in your apartment to read new emails. | Mandatory |
| **Amber's Call** | Receive the phone call or message from Amber regarding her father. | Narrative Trigger |
| **Skill Level 5** | While not strictly required, having Level 5 in cleaning efficiency is recommended. | Recommended |

> **Tip:** Always check the trash bin and the sink in your apartment between missions. Sometimes small environmental cues indicate that a new story beat is ready to trigger.

## Starting the "Amber Alert" Mission

The official start of Act 2 is widely considered to be the **Amber Alert** mission. This job takes you away from the suburban homes and into a grimier, more industrial setting. In this mission, the protagonist, Kovalsky, must help Amber clean up a mess that is "very personal." This level introduces more complex verticality and hidden areas that require the ladder or advanced jumping skills.

### Key Objectives in Amber Alert
1. **Locate the Sewer Entrance:** The mission begins near a pizza shop that leads into the drainage system.
2. **Clear the Boxing Ring:** You will find a makeshift fighting arena that served as a front for a trafficking ring.
3. **Retrieve the Evidence:** Look for documents linking "Dave" to higher-up politicians.
4. **Dispose of the Bodies:** There are several high-profile targets here that must be bagged and removed.

## Transitioning to "Secluded Retreat"

Once "Amber Alert" is finished, the plot thickens as you move toward the **Secluded Retreat** mission at Peaks Hotel. This is a significant jump in scale and introduces mechanical hazards like security robots and automated protocols. To start this portion of Act 2, you must process the evidence found in the previous mission at your apartment's desk.

| Mission Component | Details | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Peaks Hotel** | A luxury resort "rotten to the core" with high-tech security. | High |
| **Lars's Office** | The primary objective location containing the associates list. | Critical |
| **Security Room** | Must be hacked to open all doors and disable "Killer Robots." | Mandatory |
| **Senator Hullman** | Evidence found here links the politician to the "traditional values" scandal. | Story |

> **Warning:** The robots in the Secluded Retreat can significantly damage your health. Ensure you have unlocked the "Stamina" or "Health" upgrades in the skill tree before attempting a 100% perfect cleanup.

## Recommended Skills for Act 2

As you follow this **crime scene cleaner how to start act 2 guide**, you will notice that the blood splatter patterns and the amount of trash increase significantly. The basic mop and sponge will no longer suffice for the "Perfect Cleanup" rating. You should invest your earned cash into the following upgrades immediately upon starting the second act.

1. **Power Washer Pro:** Essential for the large outdoor areas of the Peaks Hotel.
2. **Deep Pockets:** Act 2 missions have more "Music CDs" and "Secrets" to collect; you need the inventory space.
3. **Advanced Hacking Tool:** Required to bypass the security protocols triggered in the later half of the game.
4. **Luminol Spray Upgrades:** The dark sewers and hotel basements make blood nearly invisible to the naked eye.

## Finding Secrets and Music CDs in Act 2

Act 2 is famous for its "Music CDs" which are often hidden behind destructible walls or in locked safes. In "Secluded Retreat," for instance, there are multiple secrets hidden in the snowy areas surrounding the hotel. To find these, look for "out of place" objects like a lone snowman or a flickering light in a maintenance shed.

In "Amber Alert," the secrets are often found by looking up. Use the yellow ladders to reach the rafters of the boxing ring. There is a hidden room behind the pizza shop's kitchen that contains a golden ticket—a key item for 100% completionists.

## Plot Summary: The Senator and the Mob

The narrative shift in Act 2 is jarring. You go from being a simple janitor for the mob to a "trash collector" who holds the fate of Senator Hullman in your hands. The dialogue between Amber and Kovalsky suggests that the human trafficking ring run by "Dave" was just a small part of a larger machine. By the time you reach Lars's office in the mountains, you aren't just cleaning blood; you are gathering the "Hand of Justice" evidence needed to either burn the system down or secure your own safety.

Following this **crime scene cleaner how to start act 2 guide** ensures you don't miss these subtle story beats. The transition is complete once you successfully exfiltrate from the Peaks Hotel with the associates list in hand.

## FAQ

**Q: Why hasn't the next mission appeared on my computer?**

A: You likely haven't interacted with all the "clues" in your apartment. Check your phone for messages from Amber or walk over to the evidence board to ensure all previous mission items are pinned.

**Q: Can I go back and finish Act 1 secrets after starting Act 2?**

A: Yes, you can use the "Mission Select" feature from the main menu or the apartment computer to replay any previous level to find missed Music CDs or secrets.

**Q: How do I handle the robots in the Secluded Retreat mission?**

A: You cannot "fight" the robots directly in a traditional sense. You must use the security room terminals to hack their logic or wait for the "Threat Eliminated" prompt to trigger after completing specific cleaning objectives.

**Q: What is the most important tool for the crime scene cleaner how to start act 2 guide?**

A: The Power Washer is your best friend in Act 2. The maps are significantly larger, and the manual mop is too slow for the expansive hotel decks and sewer tunnels.
