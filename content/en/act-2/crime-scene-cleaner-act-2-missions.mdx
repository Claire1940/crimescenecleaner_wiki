## Amber Alert: The First Challenge of Act 2

The "Amber Alert" mission is the gateway to the **crime scene cleaner act 2 missions**. Set in a multi-functional facility featuring a bar, a boxing ring, and a lounge, it presents a chaotic scene with 12 bodies scattered across various rooms. Your primary objective is to locate all victims, dispose of them, and clean the astronomical amount of blood left behind.

### Essential Keycard and Access Points

Early in the mission, you will find that several critical areas—the maintenance room, the lounge, and the boxing ring—are inaccessible. To unlock these, you must locate a specific NPC.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Body number four is the most important corpse in the mission. He holds the keycard required to access the maintenance room and the boxing ring. Do not move him until you have secured the card!

Once you have the keycard, you can open a shortcut to the starting area, which significantly reduces the time spent carrying trash bags and bodies to your vehicle.

## Advanced Cleaning Strategies for Act 2

In the **crime scene cleaner act 2 missions**, your standard mop might not be enough. The sheer volume of blood in the "Amber Alert" mission means your water will become dirty almost instantly.

1.  **Prioritize the Power Washer:** Use the power washer for large floor areas like the boxing ring and the gym. It covers more ground than the mop, though it uses more water.
2.  **Strategic Bucket Placement:** Don't leave your bucket at the entrance. Carry it to a central hub, like the bar area, to minimize travel time.
3.  **Ozone Machine Usage:** Once the physical cleaning is done, the ozone machine is vital for removing lingering odors and ensuring a 100% completion rating.
4.  **Evidence First:** Always collect small pieces of evidence (shell casings, weapons) before you start spraying water. High-pressure water can knock evidence into hard-to-reach corners.

The maintenance room, unlocked with the keycard from body #4, often contains the most valuable loot in this mission. Ensure you check every locker and shelf before finishing the job.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the keycard in the Amber Alert mission?**

A: The keycard is located on body number four. This body is typically found on the upper floor in the hallway area. You must interact with the body to retrieve the card, which unlocks the maintenance room and boxing ring.

**Q: How many bodies are there in the first of the crime scene cleaner act 2 missions?**

A: In the "Amber Alert" mission, there are a total of 12 bodies that must be located and disposed of to complete the objective.

**Q: Can I complete Act 2 missions without the power washer?**

A: While it is technically possible to use a mop for everything, it is not recommended. The large surface areas in Act 2 are designed to be cleaned more efficiently with the power washer.

**Q: What happens if I miss a single blood spot?**

A: Missing spots will lower your final rating. Use the "Highlight Vision" skill (often mapped to the 'Q' key) to see any remaining contaminants that aren't visible to the naked eye.
