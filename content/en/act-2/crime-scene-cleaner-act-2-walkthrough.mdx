## Secluded Retreat: Dealing with the Peaks Hotel

The second major hurdle in your **crime scene cleaner act 2 walkthrough** is the "Secluded Retreat" mission at the Peaks Hotel. This mission shifts the tone from urban decay to high-tech nightmare fuel. The hotel, while appearing "cozy" on the surface, is guarded by lethal security protocols and killer robots. 

In this mission, you are cleaning up after a failed negotiation involving Lars and Senator Holden. The presence of androids adds a new layer of difficulty, as you must often dismantle or dispose of "tin cans" alongside human remains. This **crime scene cleaner act 2 walkthrough** highlights the importance of hacking the security room first to open all doors and disable active threats.

### Security and Hacking Steps

1.  **Locate the Security Room:** This is your first priority to gain full access to the hotel.
2.  **Trigger the Override:** Hacking the terminal will eliminate active "threats" (robots) and unlock the guest rooms.
3.  **Clear the Entryway:** The lobby contains several "frozen" guests and robots that must be processed.
4.  **Search Lars’s Office:** Although Lars may have escaped, his office contains the "associates list" necessary for the plot.

| Item/Hazard | Description | Cleanup Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Killer Robots** | Dismantled android parts. | Must be binned or moved to the disposal zone. |
| **Snowmen** | "Mr. Cauliflower" and "Miss Carrot." | Destroy and clean the underlying blood/residue. |
| **Senator's Evidence** | Photos and recordings. | Collect as secrets to unlock the true ending. |

> **Tip:** Use your ultraviolet light frequently in the hotel rooms. The "kinky" nature of the Peaks Hotel means there are often biological stains hidden in plain sight that a standard mop will miss.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get a 100% rating in the Amber Alert mission?**

A: To achieve 100%, you must clear all blood (use the UV light to check the ceiling), dispose of all human remains in the designated zone, and collect every piece of trash, including the small pizza slices hidden in the sewer area. Following a detailed **crime scene cleaner act 2 walkthrough** will help you locate the final 1% of grime often hidden behind the boxing ring.

**Q: What is the fastest way to clean the Peaks Hotel?**

A: The fastest method is to immediately head to the security room to unlock all doors. Once unlocked, work from the top floor down. Use the power washer for the lobby and the mop for the smaller guest rooms. Don't waste time on the robots until the human remains are cleared.

**Q: Where is Lars's office in Secluded Retreat?**

A: Lars's office is located on the upper floor of the hotel, accessible through the main hallway once security protocols are disabled. Inside, you will find evidence linking the Senator to the events of Act 2.

**Q: Can I skip the body disposal and still finish the level?**

A: No. Body disposal is a primary objective. Failing to dispose of all bodies will prevent you from leaving the scene and completing your **crime scene cleaner act 2 walkthrough**.
