## Mission 1: Amber Alert Walkthrough

The "Amber Alert" mission serves as the explosive opening to Act 2. Set in a multi-level bar and underground fight club, this map features a massive body count and several complex secrets. Your primary goal is to clean the Staff Area, Bar, Office, and the central Boxing Ring.

### Key Puzzles and Secrets

To achieve 100% completion, you must interact with specific environmental objects to trigger secret passages. The first major secret involves the beer barrels located in the kitchen area. You must find the labeled barrels and attach them to the pipes in the correct sequence.

| Barrel Position | Labeled Brand |
| :--- | :--- |
| **First (Left)** | Elder Gnome Stout |
| **Second** | Monastern Trappist |
| **Third** | Iced Dwarf Pale Ale |
| **Fourth (Right)** | Iced Dwarf Pilsner |

Another tricky secret is the Dartboard Puzzle located in the bar's smoke-filled room. To open the secret passage to "Dave's Lair," you must score exactly **301 points**. 

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If you are struggling with the dartboard, aim for high-value segments like the triple 20, but ensure your final dart hits the exact number needed to reach 301. Most players find it easiest to leave exactly 1 point for the final throw.

### Office Safe and Evidence
In the upstairs office, you will encounter a locked safe. The combination is **1369**. Inside, you will find several valuables and evidence files. Don't forget to check the large desk drawer for winning scratchcards and the open safe on the opposite wall for additional papers.

## Mission 2: Secluded Retreat Guide

The "Secluded Retreat" mission takes Kovalsky to a luxury spa and cabin area that hides a dark secret. This map is heavy on environmental puzzles involving statues and hidden paths. This **crime scene cleaner act 2 guide** recommends starting with the outdoor areas before moving into the high-tech interior rooms.

### The Monkey Statue Puzzle
In the spa area, you will find pedestals for three monkey statues. These statues represent the classic "See no evil, hear no evil, speak no evil" motif. You can find two monkeys near the base of the pedestals and the third hidden under a painting of a blind samurai.

**Monkey Placement Order:**
1.  **See No Evil** (Hands over eyes)
2.  **Hear No Evil** (Hands over ears)
3.  **Speak No Evil** (Hands over mouth)

### Hidden Paths
*   **The Root Wall:** In the spa, place a ladder against the wall, climb up, and break the roots to reveal a tunnel leading to a collectible CD.
*   **The Light Beam:** Near the gate, find a torch and a shovel. Follow the direction of the light beam to find a hidden hut containing extra loot.

## Mission 3: Circle of Friends Walkthrough

"Circle of Friends" is set in Senator Holden's villa, a sprawling estate filled with high-value items and macabre secrets. This mission requires significant backtracking and parkour to find every collectible. Using a **crime scene cleaner act 2 guide** is essential here due to the verticality of the map.

### The Vampire Room Challenge
On the first floor, you will find a room themed after vampires, complete with a chained coffin. To open the coffin and claim the CD inside, you must find and destroy five black rubber ducks hidden throughout the room.

| Duck Location | Hidden Spot |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Duck 1** | Directly above the chained coffin |
| **Duck 2** | On a shelf in the same room |
| **Duck 3** | Inside a chest near the entrance |
| **Duck 4** | Bottom-left drawer of the desk |
| **Duck 5** | On the bookshelf near the record player |

### The Wine Cellar Secret
In the wine cellar, navigate to the area with the faucet. Use your mop to clear the cobwebs from the wall directly in front of the faucet. This will reveal a hidden brick that acts as a lever to open a secret room.

## Mission 4: Town Too Small Completion

The final major mission of Act 2, "Town Too Small," brings the cleanup to a local bowling alley and kitchen. While smaller than the villa, it contains several "blink-and-you-miss-it" items.

1.  **The Kitchen Vent:** The kitchen door is initially blocked by trash. Once cleared, go outside and look for a breakable grate. Inside, you will find a CD resting on a bed.
2.  **The Bowling Alley Handle:** Go to the room behind the bowling lanes. On a desk, you will find a door handle. Take this handle to the maintenance room (climb over the wooden boxes) and attach it to the door to unlock the final secret area.

## New Customization and Skills

Act 2 doesn't just add missions; it expands the gameplay mechanics. Two new abilities have been added to the **General** category of the skill tree: **Pat on the Back 1** and **Pat on the Back 2**. These skills provide passive bonuses that make high-tier cleanups more efficient.

You can now unlock several new skins for your equipment. Many of these are purchased with points earned during missions, but the most prestigious are the "True Cleaner" skins.

| Equipment | Unlock Method |

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock Act 2 in Crime Scene Cleaner?**

A: To begin Act 2, you must first complete all Act 1 missions. Once finished, look for the mission titled "Go to sleep" in the top left corner of your map. This prologue leads directly into the Amber Alert mission.

**Q: What is the safe code for the Amber Alert office?**

A: The safe code is **1369**. You can enter this by clicking the numbers as the slider passes them or typing them in directly if your skill tree allows.

**Q: Why can't I get 100% on the trash objective?**

A: As noted in our **crime scene cleaner act 2 guide**, Act 2 introduces very small trash items like shurikens, darts, and bullets stuck in walls. Use your "Cleaner Sense" (Flashlight/Vision) frequently to highlight these tiny objects that are often missed in dark corners.

**Q: Is the CD achievement bugged in Act 2?**

A: Some players report missing one CD even after following guides. Ensure you have collected the unique CD located in Kovalsky's house during the Act 2 prologue. If you have that and still lack the achievement, check the "Community Update" maps, as those CDs also count toward the total.
