## Essential Cleaning Tools and Equipment

Success in the **crime scene cleaner chapter list** depends heavily on your mastery of the tools provided. Each tool has a specific use case, and using the wrong one can lead to "tracking" blood across a clean floor.

### The Mop and Bucket
The mop is your primary tool for large floor surfaces. However, it requires constant maintenance. As the mop icon in the bottom right turns red, you must rinse it in your bucket. If you continue mopping with a dirty tool, you will leave streaks, increasing your workload.

### Sponges and Detergents
Sponges are best used for vertical surfaces like walls, curtains, and furniture. Detergents come in different "strengths." While standard water works for hardwood, you will need "Strength Detergent" for carpets and rugs to remove deep-set stains.

### The Trash Can and Body Bags
Every broken item, pizza box, and bottle counts toward your trash percentage. Bodies must be bagged and transported to your truck. In many levels, like "Bad Call," you can use environmental shortcuts like elevators or windows to toss bags down to the street level, saving you dozens of trips up and down stairs.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when placing your bucket. If you sprint into it or drop it on uneven ground, it will spill, creating a new mess that you must clean up, potentially ruining a "Smooth Operator" run.

## Advanced Strategies for 100% Completion

To truly master the **crime scene cleaner chapter list**, you need to look beyond the surface level. The game tracks "Deception," which is your ability to make the scene look as if nothing ever happened.

### Blood Vision (The Y Button)
Always utilize your "Sense" or "Blood Vision" by pressing the Y button. This highlights tiny specks of blood that are invisible to the naked eye. These specks often hide under furniture, behind doors, or on the ceiling.

### The "Smooth Operator" Achievement
This is one of the most difficult challenges in the game, requiring you to complete a mission without breaking any items or creating new stains (like spilling your bucket). 
*   **Pro Tip:** There is a known strategy where saving and exiting to the main menu just before leaving in the truck can sometimes "reset" accidental breaks, allowing the achievement to pop upon completion.

### Furniture Restoration
A common mistake is forgetting to put the furniture back. Once a room is clean, use your "Sense" to see "ghost" outlines of where chairs, tables, and cushions belong. You must hold the interaction button to snap them back into their original positions.

## Upgrading Kowalsky’s Skills

As you collect cassettes and find secrets throughout the various missions, you earn skill points. These should be invested wisely to make later, more difficult chapters manageable.

| Skill Category | Benefit | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cleaner Capacity** | Carry more detergent and bags. | **High** |
| **Mop Reach** | Clean larger areas with one swipe. | **Medium** |
| **Sprinting** | Move faster while carrying heavy items. | **Low** |
| **Detection** | Highlights blood from further away. | **High** |
| **Bucket Stability** | Reduces the chance of spilling. | **Medium** |

Investing in Detection early on is highly recommended, as it significantly reduces the time spent hunting for that final 1% of blood in larger levels like the Nightclub or the Art Gallery.

## FAQ

**Q: How many chapters are currently in the Crime Scene Cleaner chapter list?**

A: As of the 2026 update, there are 10 primary story chapters, along with several side "contract" missions that players can take on to earn extra cash for upgrades.

**Q: Can I replay missions to find missed secrets?**

A: Yes, you can access the mission select screen from Kowalsky's computer in his home. Replaying levels allows you to find missed cassettes and evidence without losing your overall story progress.

**Q: What happens if I leave a crime scene with only 95% of the blood cleaned?**

A: You will still complete the mission, but your "Deception" score will be lower, resulting in significantly less pay and fewer skill points. To get the best ending and all achievements, 100% is the goal.

**Q: Is there a way to carry more than one trash bag at a time?**

A: Initially, no. However, by upgrading your "Cleaner Capacity" in the skill tree, you can eventually unlock the ability to carry multiple bags and even larger trash bins, making the cleanup process much more efficient.
