## Mission Overview: The White Peaks Incident

The Secluded Retreat mission begins with an eerie arrival at the White Peaks Hotel. Your contact, Amber, informs you that the hotel's security protocols have been triggered, leading to a lethal "man versus machine" scenario. Your primary objective is to clean the lobby, guest rooms, and the high-tech "playrooms" where things went horribly wrong.

## Hacking the Security Room

Before you can access the majority of the hotel, you must deal with the security lockout. The hotel is under a "threat eliminated" protocol, but many doors remain sealed until you manually override the system.

1.  **Locate the Security Hub:** Follow the blood trails in the main lobby toward the staff service area.
2.  **Interact with the Terminal:** You will need to complete a short hacking minigame to open the guest room doors.
3.  **Monitor the Cameras:** Use the monitors to identify the rooms with the highest concentration of "trash" to plan your route efficiently.

Once the doors are open, the true scale of the cleanup becomes apparent. The hotel serves as a "peaceful retreat" for the wealthy, but the presence of killer robots and exploding machines has turned it into a slaughterhouse.

## Mastering the Crime Scene Cleaner Secluded Retreat Guide: Cleaning Strategies

Cleaning the White Peaks Hotel requires a different approach than previous levels. You aren't just dealing with blood; you are dealing with hydraulic fluid, synthetic parts, and the aftermath of high-tech malfunctions.

### Dealing with Android Remains
Unlike human bodies, android remains can be heavy and awkward to carry. Some robots have exploded, leaving small, sharp shrapnel across the floor. Use your vacuum or a specialized broom to gather these small parts before they puncture your trash bags.

### Managing Large Areas
The lobby and the snowy exterior are vast. It is recommended to use the **Ozone Machine** in the smaller guest rooms while you manually scrub the larger open spaces. This allows you to multitask and significantly reduces your completion time.

| Tool | Best Use Case in Secluded Retreat | Effectiveness |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Power Washer** | Outdoor snow areas and large lobby blood pools | ★★★★★ |
| **Sponge** | Delicate furniture in Lars's office | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Ozone Machine** | Removing the "nightmare fuel" scent from guest rooms | ★★★★☆ |
| **Trash Bin** | Collecting robot limbs and broken "toys" | ★★★★★ |

## Secret Locations and Music CDs

To achieve a 100% completion rating, you must find the hidden collectibles scattered throughout the hotel. These items are often tucked away in corners that aren't immediately obvious during a standard cleaning pass.

*   **Music CD 1:** Located in the lobby's reception desk, hidden behind a stack of guest logs.
*   **Music CD 2:** Found in the "playroom" area, near the cages. It is often obscured by broken glass.
*   **Cassette Tape:** Look inside Lars's office, specifically on the bookshelf near the window he allegedly escaped through.
*   **Secret Room:** Behind the bar in the lounge, there is a loose panel that leads to a small storage area containing extra cleaning supplies and a hidden trophy.

> **💡 Tip:** Use your "Cleaner Sense" (highlighting tool) frequently. The white snow outside can make it difficult to see small pieces of evidence or glass shards.

## Finding Lars's Office and the Associates List

The narrative climax of the Secluded Retreat involves uncovering the connection between Lars and Senator Hullman. As you clean, you will eventually reach Lars's private office. 

Lars is not present—Amber suggests he may have escaped through the window—but he left behind a wealth of incriminating data. Your goal is to find the **Associates List**. This document is vital for the story progression and confirms that the Senator, a "guardian of traditional values," is deeply involved in the hotel's illicit activities. 

Before leaving, ensure you have checked the following in the office:
1.  The desk drawers for the list.
2.  The computer terminal for lore-building emails.
3.  The floor for any "reflective" mirrors that need to be cleaned or disposed of.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I open the locked doors in the Secluded Retreat?**

A: You must find the Security Room located near the back of the lobby. Interact with the main terminal to trigger a "threat eliminated" broadcast, which will unlock all guest room doors and the elevator.

**Q: Where is the best place to dump bodies in the White Peaks Hotel?**

A: There is a designated disposal chute near the service entrance, or you can carry them back to your van. Given the size of the map, using the chute is much more efficient for this **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat guide**.

**Q: I can't find the last 1% of blood. Where could it be?**

A: Check the ceiling in the "Man versus Machine" room. Often, when the robots malfunctioned, blood was sprayed upward. Also, check under the rugs in the guest suites, as liquid often seeps beneath them.

**Q: Is there a time limit for the Secluded Retreat mission?**

A: No, there is no strict time limit. However, completing the mission faster and with fewer broken items will result in a higher payout and better reputation gain in the 2026 campaign mode.
