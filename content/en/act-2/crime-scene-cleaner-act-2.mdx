## Amber Alert Walkthrough: The First Major Challenge

The "Amber Alert" mission serves as the true introduction to the high-stakes world of Act 2. You are tasked by Amber, Big Jim’s daughter, to clean up a brutal scene at an underground fight club. With 12 bodies to dispose of and a massive surface area, efficiency is key.

### Phase 1: The Staff Area and Kitchen
Upon entering, head to the Staff Area on the right. You will find your first body and a valuable ring in the lockers. After clearing the blood, move to the kitchen. Here, you will encounter the first major puzzle involving beer barrels.

To unlock the first secret room in the kitchen, you must attach four specific barrels to the pipes in the correct order. 

**Kitchen Barrel Puzzle Order (Left to Right):**
1. **Elder Gnome Stout** (The only one with a clear label near the door).
2. **Monastern Trappist**.
3. **Iced Dwarf Pale Ale**.
4. **Iced Dwarf Pilsner**.

Completing this opens a compartment containing the first collectible CD of the mission.

### Phase 2: The Bar and the Dartboard Secret
The main bar area is filled with broken glass and furniture that must be reset. However, the most elusive secret is hidden behind the dartboard. To access "Dave’s Lair," you must interact with the dartboard and score a specific point total.

| Puzzle | Requirement | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dartboard Challenge** | Score exactly **301** | Ladder to Dave's Lair / Turtle Bucket Skin |
| **Office Safe** | Code: **1369** | High-value loot & Evidence |
| **Boxing Ring Access** | Maintenance Terminal | Access to the final cleaning area |

## Secrets and Collectibles in Act 2

The **crime scene cleaner act 2** update has significantly increased the number of hidden trophies and CDs. Many of these require "parkour" or interacting with the environment in non-obvious ways, such as walking through "fake" walls or breaking roots in the spa area.

### CD and Skin Locations
*   **Turtle Bucket Skin:** Found in the secret room behind the dartboard in Amber Alert.
*   **Vampire Room CD:** In the "Circle of Friends" villa, you must find and destroy five black rubber ducks hidden around the room with the chained coffin to unlock the reward.
*   **Spa CD:** In "Secluded Retreat," bring a ladder to the spa area, climb behind the structure, and break the roots blocking the tunnel.
*   **Painting Secret:** In the Lounges area of Amber Alert, look for a large painting of ruins. You can literally walk through the canvas to find a hidden room with a CD and three valuable cat statues.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always check under bodies before disposing of them. In Act 2, several key items, like the VIP Pass in the bar, are physically obscured by corpses.

## Advanced Cleaning Strategies for Act 2

The environments in Act 2 are much more prone to "secondary messes." Breaking glass bottles or knocking over buckets will create additional work that can prevent a 100% score. 

1. **Clear the Path First:** Before moving bodies to the truck, clear all furniture and large trash from the hallways. The "Huge" map sizes make backtracking for a spilled bucket extremely time-consuming.
2. **Use the Flashlight/Scanner Constantly:** Act 2 features darker environments like the "Dave's Lair" and the "Town Too Small" maintenance tunnels. Tiny pieces of evidence like matchboxes or toothpicks are easily missed without the scanner.
3. **Evidence Priority:** Some evidence items are "weapons" lodged in walls or bodies. In the "Lounges" section of Amber Alert, you must find two separate Sais—one in the door and one inside a body—to complete the collection.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock Act 2 in Crime Scene Cleaner?**

A: You must complete all Act 1 missions and the "Things Left Behind" prologue. Once you go to sleep in Kovalsky's house after Elena's party, the map will update with the new Act 2 locations.

**Q: I'm missing one CD in Act 2, where could it be?**

A: Many players miss the CD located in the "Act 2 Prologue" (Kovalsky's house). If you have that one, check the "Town Too Small" kitchen vent or the "Circle of Friends" wine cellar secret brick.

**Q: What is the "True Cleaner" mode in Act 2?**

A: True Cleaner is an increased difficulty mode unlocked after completing a map with a perfect score. Completing Act 2 missions on True Cleaner rewards you with exclusive "Golden" skins for your tools.

**Q: How do I score 301 on the dartboard in Amber Alert?**

A: You need to hit high-value targets like the triple-20 or the bullseye. If you are struggling, try to get close to the number and use the final dart to hit a single-digit segment to reach exactly 301.
