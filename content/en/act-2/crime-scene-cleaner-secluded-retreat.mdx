The **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat** mission is the third major assignment in Act 2, taking players to the opulent but blood-stained White Peaks hotel. This mission is significantly more complex than previous levels, featuring a massive three-story layout, 11 bodies to dispose of, and a variety of high-tech and themed rooms that require specific keycards to access. To achieve a perfect score in the **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat** level, you must not only mop up every drop of blood but also solve environmental puzzles and locate hidden collectibles that are easily missed in the dark.

In this guide, we will walk you through the essential steps to restore power, locate every piece of evidence, and uncover the secrets hidden within Lars Mattson’s "exclusive" getaway. Whether you are aiming for the "True Cleaner" achievement or just trying to find that last elusive CD, this walkthrough has you covered.

## Mission Overview and Initial Objectives

When you first arrive at the White Peaks hotel, the power is out, and the entire lobby is bathed in an eerie red emergency light. You cannot use the elevator or access certain electronic locks until the main breakers are flipped.

| Objective | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **People Count** | 11 Bodies |
| **Location Size** | Level 3 (Large) |
| **Evidence Pieces** | 9 Total |
| **Keycards Needed** | 4 (VIP, 01, 02, 03) |
| **Collectibles** | 3 Cassette CDs |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not attempt to clean in the dark. Your first priority should be reaching the Security Room to restore power, which makes blood stains much more visible and unlocks the shutters.

## Step 0: Restoring the Main Power

From the lobby, you must navigate the darkened corridors to reach the Security Room. Head to the right of the reception desk and crouch under the half-open shutter. You will pass a deactivated turret—don't worry, it won't fire yet. Follow the stairs down to the bar area where a robot bartender stands idle. 

Go behind the bar into the Maintenance Room, then cross into the Relaxation Zone. Look for passages with broken shutters and follow them until you reach the corridor containing the Security Room. Flip the large power switch inside. Once the lights are on, the hotel's "peaceful" atmosphere returns, and you can begin the real work of cleaning.

## Keycard Locations and Room Access

The **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat** mission uses a tiered access system. You cannot enter the themed suites on the first floor without finding the corresponding cards scattered throughout the ground floor and basement.

| Keycard | Location | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| **VIP Keycard** | Maintenance Room (on the workbench) | The Office (First Floor) |
| **Keycard 01** | Restaurant (on a dinner table) | Room #1 (Sex Dungeon) |
| **Keycard 02** | First Floor Corridor (near Body #8) | Room #2 (Spaceship) |
| **Keycard 03** | Gentlemen’s Club (table near white chairs) | Room #3 (Medieval Fantasy) |

## Detailed Cleaning Checklist by Area

### 1. The Lobby and Lounges
The lobby contains the first piece of evidence: a **Robotic Hand with a Gun** located behind the reception counter. You’ll also need to move the luggage cart and various furniture back to their highlighted silhouettes. 

*   **Body #1:** Found stretched out on a table in the Lounge area.
*   **Valuables:** Check the wooden wardrobe for a necklace and the security desk for $100.

### 2. The Relaxation Zone (Spa & Pool)
This area is one of the most time-consuming due to the water and the verticality of the pool.

*   **Body #2:** Located on a massage table in the massage room.
*   **Body #3:** Found inside a meditation pod in the Yoga Room.
*   **Evidence:** A phone sitting on a table in the changing room next to the pool.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The pool area has several "hidden" blood splatters on the decorative rocks. Use your ultraviolet lamp thoroughly here to ensure 100% completion.

### 3. The Restaurant and Kitchen
The restaurant is a mess of overturned chairs and broken glass. You need to return 19 pieces of furniture to their original spots here.

*   **Body #6:** Hanging from the chandelier. You must throw an object (like a bottle or trash) at it to knock it down.
*   **Body #7:** Located on the stove in the kitchen.
*   **Evidence:** A bloody cleaver found directly on Body #7.

## Solving the Secret Puzzles

The **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat** features two major environmental puzzles that reward players with secret CDs and trophies.

### The Monkey Statue Puzzle
In the Relaxation Zone's Yoga Room, you will find five pedestals. You must find and place the "See No Evil, Hear No Evil, Speak No Evil" monkeys in the correct order based on hints found in paintings throughout the hotel.

1.  **Blue Monkey:** Ring in mouth.
2.  **Grey Monkey:** Neutral pose.
3.  **Green Monkey:** Neutral pose.
4.  **Purple Monkey:** Wearing sunglasses.
5.  **Yellow Monkey:** Wearing headphones.

Once placed, a secret door opens to a red room containing the cassette **"Blue Assemble – When December Turns Blue."**

### The Spaceship Panel Puzzle
In Room #2 (The Spaceship Suite), there is a 4x3 grid of buttons near the surgical table. To unlock the secret compartment, press the buttons in the following sequence: **1 – 6 – 11 – 12** (counting from top-left to bottom-right). This reveals the **"Atarak – UFO Porno"** cassette.

## Collectible CD Locations

For completionists, finding all CDs is mandatory. Here is where they are hidden in the **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat** map:

1.  **CD #1 (Spa Tunnel):** In the spa area, find a ladder and place it against the wall to reach the roots behind it. Break the roots and follow the tunnel to the end.
2.  **CD #2 (Mirror Room):** On the second floor, enter the "unusual room" at the end of the corridor past the piano. The CD is near the mirror.
3.  **CD #3 (Monkey Secret):** Found on a tray behind a mannequin inside the secret room unlocked by the Monkey Puzzle.

## The Forest Path Secret
Before you leave in your truck, head toward the courtyard gate. You will see a flashlight shining into some bushes. Crouch and follow the hidden path through the snow. This leads to a greenhouse containing the **Gardener’s Recording**, a big pack of drugs, and a "Money Tree" that provides a significant cash boost.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing on "True Cleaner" mode, remember that you cannot leave any bodies behind. If you are struggling to hit 100% cleanliness, check the ceilings—blood often splatters upward during the more violent animations.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the body down from the chandelier in the restaurant?**

A: You can pick up any small piece of trash or a bottle from the floor and throw it at the body. A single hit should cause it to fall so you can bag it and take it to the truck.

**Q: I’m missing one piece of evidence in the Office. Where is it?**

A: There are two pieces in the Office. One is a photo above the safe, and the second is Lars's Phone, which is *inside* the safe. You must hack the safe to collect it.

**Q: Why can't I find the last 1% of trash in the Secluded Retreat?**

A: The **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat** map is notorious for small items like shurikens, darts, and bullet casings. Check the floor of the Spaceship room and the "Sex Dungeon" room very carefully, as these small items often blend into the carpet.

**Q: Where do I find the missing red cushion for the lobby chair?**

A: The red square cushion is actually located in the Gentlemen’s Club at the bottom of the stairs. You must carry it all the way back to the lobby to complete the furniture objective.
