## The Alter Ego Laboratory Puzzle

The "Alter Ego" mission introduces a more sterile, yet far more dangerous, environment. The highlight of this mission is the Laboratory section, which requires you to locate and install four fuses to activate the facility's machinery. This area is a significant step up in complexity compared to earlier levels in the **crime scene cleaner new update guide 2026**.

### How to Find the 4 Fuses
To progress through the laboratory, you must find four specific fuses scattered across different levels of the facility. Some require simple exploration, while others involve environmental parkour and electrical puzzles.

1.  **Fuse #1 (The Entry Fuse):** This is found directly in the main room with the four sockets. It is the easiest to locate and serves as your introduction to the mechanic.
2.  **Fuse #2 (The Ladder Climb):** Locate the area with the garbage bins. You will need to move a body or a large item to use as a platform. Jump onto the item, crouch-jump to reach the ladder, and climb up to find the second fuse on the upper walkway.
3.  **Fuse #3 (The Electric Chair Puzzle):** This is the most difficult fuse to obtain. You must find an electrical component on the upper floor (accessible via the elevator). Bring this component back down and plug it into the wall near the electric chair. Place the fuse box over the metal detector to avoid triggering the security door, then activate the chair to pop the box open.
4.  **Fuse #4 (The Upper Table):** Take the elevator to the top floor and head to the left. Walk across the upper walkway to find the final fuse sitting on a table. You can safely jump down from here to save time.

## Advanced Cleaning Techniques for 2026

The 2026 update has refined the physics of cleaning, meaning your choice of tool matters more than ever. While the power washer is great for outdoor areas, indoor residential and laboratory settings require a more nuanced approach.

### Tool Synergy and Efficiency
Using the right tool for the right surface can reduce your mission time by up to 30%. In the "Bad Call" mission, for example, the walls are often covered in grime that the mop simply moves around. Switching to a sponge allows for precision cleaning that ensures no "ghost stains" remain when you check your UV light.

*   **The Mop:** Best for large, flat floor surfaces. Ensure you change your water frequently to avoid spreading blood.
*   **The Sponge:** Essential for furniture, tight corners, and vertical surfaces. It absorbs more liquid per swipe but requires more frequent rinsing.
*   **The Power Washer:** Reserved for heavy-duty exterior cleaning. Be careful using this indoors as it can knock over physics objects, creating more of a mess to organize later.

> **💡 Tip:** Always dispose of bodies before starting the deep clean. Dragging a body across a clean floor will leave new trails, forcing you to redo your work.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I open the locked box in the Alter Ego mission?**

A: To open the box containing the third fuse, you must find the electrical component on the upper floor, bring it to the electric chair room, and power the chair. Make sure to move the box past the metal detector first to ensure the door doesn't lock you out.

**Q: What is the best way to clean blood on carpets in the 2026 update?**

A: Carpets now require a two-step process. First, use the vacuum or a dry sponge to soak up the excess, then apply cleaning detergent before using the mop. This prevents the stain from setting and becoming permanent.

**Q: Where can I find the key to Apartment 23 in the Bad Call mission?**

A: The key is hidden near the entrance of the apartment. Check the immediate vicinity of the door frame or nearby small furniture. The client usually leaves it in a "hidden but obvious" spot as per the mission dialogue.
