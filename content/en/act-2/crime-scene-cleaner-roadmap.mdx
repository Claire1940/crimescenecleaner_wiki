## Act II: New Narrative Missions

The **crime scene cleaner roadmap** places a heavy emphasis on storytelling. Act II is set to expand the world significantly, moving beyond the initial missions to explore higher-stakes environments. Two of the most anticipated additions include the Underground Lab and the GameDev Office.

### The Underground Lab (Community Contest Winner)
The Underground Lab is a special addition to the game, born from a community contest held on the official Discord. Players submitted their most creative (and gruesome) ideas for a crime scene, and the "Underground Lab" took the top spot. This map features:
*   **Complex Biohazards:** New types of spills that require specialized cleaning agents.
*   **High-Tech Evidence:** Unique items that must be disposed of or hacked to clear the scene.
*   **Verticality:** Multiple levels of catwalks and vats that make disposal more challenging.

### The GameDev Office
In a humorous yet dark twist, the developers are adding a GameDev Office map. This level serves as a meta-commentary on the industry while providing a dense, cluttered environment that is a nightmare for anyone trying to achieve a 100% cleanliness rating. Expect to find plenty of Easter eggs hidden among the overturned desks and shattered monitors.

## Kovalsky’s Workshop Upgrade

Your hideout is no longer just a place to select missions. As part of the ongoing updates, Kovalsky’s workshop has received a massive overhaul. This new room serves as a central hub where you can:
1.  **Admire Your Loadout:** View all your unlocked gear and tools in high detail.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When entering the new workshop for the first time, ensure your game is fully updated to avoid clipping issues with displayed trophies.

## Accessing the Beta: A Step-by-Step Guide

For players who can't wait for the official release of Act II, the developers often host beta branches on Steam. This allows you to test the **crime scene cleaner roadmap** content early, though it comes with the risk of bugs and unfinished assets.

| Step | Action | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Step 1** | **Backup Saves** | Copy your save files to a safe folder on your desktop. Beta progress can corrupt main files. |
| **Step 2** | Properties | Right-click *Crime Scene Cleaner* in your Steam Library and select **Properties**. |
| **Step 3** | Betas Tab | Navigate to the "Betas" tab on the left-hand menu. |
| **Step 4** | Enter Code | Input the beta access code (available on the official Discord) into the "Private Beta" field. |
| **Step 5** | Update | Allow Steam to download the beta branch files. |

The developers strongly emphasize the importance of backing up your data. Since the beta introduces maps like the Lab and the GameDev Office (which may be numbered 10 and 11 in the sequence), jumping back to the main branch without a backup could result in losing your campaign progress.
