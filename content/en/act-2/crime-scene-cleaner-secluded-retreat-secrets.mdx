## Navigating the Security Protocols

The Secluded Retreat isn't just a cleaning job; it's a puzzle. When you first arrive, many areas are locked down. You must find the security room to gain full access to the building. This is a critical step in finding all **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat secrets**, as several secret rooms are behind doors that only open once the "threat has been eliminated" via the security console.

Once the doors are open, you can explore the snowy exterior paths and the upper balconies. Keep an eye out for the "Man versus Machine" scenes. These areas are high-density mess zones, but they often contain the tools you need, such as extra trash bags or cleaning chemicals, hidden behind crates.

## Lars's Office and the Associates List

The climax of the mission involves infiltrating Lars's office. While the man himself has escaped through a window, he left behind a treasure trove of evidence. This is where the narrative secrets of the Secluded Retreat come to light.

*   **The Associates List:** This is your primary objective in the office. It links the hotel's activities to high-profile figures.
*   **The Senator Hullman Connection:** By interacting with the computer or finding specific notes, you discover that the "guardian of traditional values" is deeply involved in the hotel's darker side.
*   **Hidden Safes:** Check behind paintings or under the desk for small safes that contain extra cash and lore documents.

> **Tip:** Don't leave the office until you've checked every drawer. The associates list is mandatory, but the extra lore documents contribute to your final "Discovery" score.

## Advanced Cleaning Strategies for the Peaks Hotel

The Secluded Retreat introduces "tin cans" (androids) that must be disposed of. Unlike human bodies, these can sometimes be heavy or require you to break them down into smaller pieces before they fit into disposal bins.

1.  **Manage Your Bag Capacity:** As the cleaner notes, you "can't pick up anything with a full bag." Constantly cycle back to your disposal point or use the hotel's service elevators to move large quantities of trash.
2.  **The Mirror Problem:** The cleaner mentions not being a fan of mirrors because they are "too reflective." In gameplay terms, mirrors show missed spots very clearly. Use the hotel's many mirrors to check your character's back for blood splashes or to see reflections of messes on the ceiling.
3.  **Snow and Blood:** Cleaning the snowy areas outside is tricky. The blood can seep into the snow, requiring more water than usual. Use the "Heat" stations if available to melt stubborn ice patches that might be hiding evidence.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the most commonly missed secret in the Secluded Retreat?**

A: Most players miss the Music CD hidden in the maintenance tunnels behind the exploding machinery. You have to wait for the steam to clear or find a side passage to reach it safely. Finding these **crime scene cleaner secluded retreat secrets** requires patience and careful timing.

**Q: How do I open the locked doors in the guest wing?**

A: You must find the security room, usually located near the central hub of the hotel. Once you interact with the main console and "eliminate the threat" (disable the lockdown), the hotel AI will announce that all doors are now open.

**Q: Is there a way to deal with the robots without getting hurt?**

A: In the Secluded Retreat, most robots are already "dead" or malfunctioning. However, some environmental hazards triggered by the robots (like electrical leaks) can damage you. Always look for the yellow power boxes to shut down local grids before cleaning near robot remains.

**Q: Can I complete the mission without finding Lars's associates list?**

A: No, the associates list is a mandatory objective for Act 2. While you can skip some of the hidden music CDs, the list is required to trigger the final dialogue regarding Senator Hullman and complete the level.
