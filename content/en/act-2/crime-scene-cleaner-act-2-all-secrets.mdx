## Amber Alert: Sewer Secrets and Hidden Evidence

The "Amber Alert" mission is a descent into a literal and figurative sewer. While the primary goal is to clean up the aftermath of a violent confrontation involving Amber and a trafficking ring, the environment is packed with collectibles.

### The Sewer Ninja Easter Egg
One of the most popular secrets in this level is the nod to a famous quartet of pizza-loving warriors. Deep within the sewer tunnels, look for a secluded alcove that smells remarkably like pepperoni. You will find a pizza box and a "wise rat" setup, which serves as a fun break from the otherwise grim atmosphere.

### The Trafficking Evidence
To fully uncover the **crime scene cleaner act 2 all secrets** in this level, you must locate the hidden documents connecting the club owner, Dave, to Senator Hollman. 

1.  **The Golden Ticket:** Found in the VIP locker area. This item is crucial for unlocking the full story context.
2.  **The Senator's Recordings:** Located in a small office tucked behind the main bar area. You may need to move several crates to reveal the floor safe.
3.  **The Changing Rooms:** Check both the "Winners" and "Losers" locker rooms. There is a music CD hidden on top of the lockers in the losers' section.

## Secluded Retreat: Peaks Hotel Secrets

"Secluded Retreat" takes you to the snowy Peaks Hotel. This mission is unique because it features "killer robots" and high-tech security protocols that can make cleaning a hazardous task.

### Accessing the Security Room
Upon arrival, the hotel's security protocols will likely be active. To find the security room—which counts as a major secret area—you need to navigate to the maintenance wing. Hacking the terminal here not only opens all doors for easier cleaning but also reveals a hidden stash of cleaning supplies and a collectible cassette.

### Lars's Office and the Escape Route
The office of Lars is a central hub for evidence. While the target might have escaped through the window, he left behind a trail of secrets.

*   **The Associates List:** Check the desk drawers thoroughly. This list links the hotel's "kinky" activities back to the central antagonists.
*   **The Hidden Safe:** Behind the large painting in the office is a safe. The code can be found on a sticky note under the keyboard in the reception area.
*   **The Music CD:** Located on the balcony just outside the office window. It’s easy to miss if you don't step out into the snow.

## Collectible Music CDs in Act 2

Finding all music CDs is a secondary objective that rewards players with new tracks to listen to during their shifts. In Act 2, these are often placed in high-up locations or behind "interactable" scenery.

1.  **Sewer CD (Amber Alert):** Located in the drainage pipe near the initial spawn. You have to crouch and walk into the pipe to find it.
2.  **Club CD (Amber Alert):** Hidden inside a broken speaker near the DJ booth. You must smash the speaker to grab it.
3.  **Mountain CD (Secluded Retreat):** Found in the "Zen Garden" area of the hotel, tucked under a stone bench.
4.  **Lobby CD (Secluded Retreat):** On the chandelier in the main lobby. You will need to use a ladder or jump from the second-floor railing to reach it.

> **Warning:** Be careful when jumping for collectibles in the Peaks Hotel. The fall damage is significant, and dying will reset your cleaning progress for that room.

## Advanced Cleaning Tips for Act 2

As you pursue **crime scene cleaner act 2 all secrets**, the actual cleaning becomes more difficult. Use these strategies to maintain your efficiency:

*   **Handle the Androids First:** In "Secluded Retreat," the destroyed androids leak a specific type of synthetic oil that requires a heavy-duty detergent. Don't waste your standard soap on these stains.
*   **The Sewer Smell:** In "Amber Alert," the environmental grime can mask actual bloodstains. Use your UV lamp constantly to distinguish between "sewer sludge" and "target blood."
*   **Body Disposal:** In the Peaks Hotel, use the service elevator to move bodies to the incinerator or your truck. Carrying them through the lobby is slow and risks leaving a trail of blood on clean carpets.
