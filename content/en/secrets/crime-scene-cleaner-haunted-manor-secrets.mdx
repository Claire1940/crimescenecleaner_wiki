## Mission Overview: Short Circuit

In this mission, you arrive at a luxury smart home that has been compromised by a malicious hacker. The resulting chaos led the residents to believe they were being haunted, eventually resulting in a tragic "short circuit" of their sanity and lives. Your job is to dispose of three bodies, erase surveillance data, and scrub every inch of the property.

The manor is divided into two main floors. Initially, much of the ground floor is locked, requiring you to head upstairs to find the necessary keys. The atmosphere is heavy with "jump scares" triggered by the house's smart systems, but as a professional cleaner, your focus remains on the grime and the hidden loot.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't let the flickering lights or sudden noises distract you. Most "supernatural" occurrences in this level are programmed responses from the hacked smart home system.

## Uncovering Crime Scene Cleaner Haunted Manor Secrets

There are two primary **crime scene cleaner haunted manor secrets** that involve hidden rooms. These areas contain significant loot and environmental storytelling elements that explain the family's descent into madness.

### Secret #1: The Hidden Gallery
To find the first secret, head to the upstairs hallway near the workshop. Look for a **wall sconce (light fixture) that appears crooked** or tilted. Interacting with this light acts as a switch. Once flipped, a section of the wall near the stacked boxes will slide open, revealing a hidden room containing a large painting and several high-value loot items.

### Secret #2: The Room Under the Stairs
The second of the **crime scene cleaner haunted manor secrets** is a direct nod to pop culture. After unlocking the Living Room on the ground floor, head toward the kitchen area near the refrigerator. There is a small, unassuming door built into the side of the staircase. Open it to find a cramped living space reminiscent of Harry Potter’s cupboard. Inside, you will find a parody book titled *Harry the Potter and the Goblet of Clay*.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be sure to check the ceiling in the secret rooms. Blood splatter often hides in the corners of these tight spaces, which can prevent you from hitting a 100% cleanliness rating.

## Cassette Tapes and Collectibles

Collectibles in the Haunted Manor are uniquely tied to the "ghostly" theme of the mission. There are two cassette tapes to find, but the second one requires a specific sequence of actions to spawn.

1.  **Cassette Tape #1:** Located upstairs. From the stairs, turn left and enter the second door on your right. The tape is sitting on a shelf against the left wall.
2.  **Cassette Tape #2:** This is a "ghost" tape. After picking up the first one, you must explore the rest of the house and attempt to interact with the locked doors downstairs. Once you have "checked" the house, return to the exact same shelf where you found the first tape. The music in the room will change, and the second tape will have manifested in the same spot.

### Special Collectibles
*   **Holy Wota:** Found on the shelves in the upstairs lounge.
*   **Girampa Girampas:** This collectible appears in the lounge (where the Holy Wota was) only after you have physically entered every single room and secret area in the manor.

## Final Cleanup Tips

To ensure you leave the manor in pristine condition and secure your bonus, keep these final tasks in mind:

*   **Destroy the Robot Vacuum:** The smart vacuum is malfunctioning and will spread blood around if left alone. Approach it and use your melee attack (punch) repeatedly until it is destroyed.
*   **Check for Paw Prints:** The "ghostly" footprints are scattered across both floors. They are faint and often lead toward the walls or under furniture. Use your UV light extensively.
*   **Furniture Alignment:** There are 29 pieces of furniture (21 downstairs, 8 upstairs) that must be returned to their original positions. Look for the yellow ghost outlines to guide you.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I find the second cassette tape in the Haunted Manor?**

A: The second tape only spawns after you have picked up the first one and explored the house. You must walk through the rooms and try the locked doors downstairs. When the background music shifts, return to the shelf where the first tape was located to find the second one.

**Q: How do I open the secret room upstairs?**

A: Look for a crooked wall lamp (sconce) in the upstairs hallway. Interact with it to toggle the hidden door located behind the nearby boxes. This is one of the most common **crime scene cleaner haunted manor secrets** players miss.

**Q: What do I do with the robot vacuum?**

A: You must destroy it. It is considered part of the "Short Circuit" malfunction. Punching it until it breaks will stop it from smearing blood and count toward your mission objectives.

**Q: Where is the Living Room key?**

A: The key is located on the first floor (upstairs). Go to the end of the hallway to the workshop/office area. The key is sitting on a table inside that room.
