## Essential Tools for the Bowling Alley

Before diving into the walkthrough, ensure your van is stocked with the right equipment. The bowling alley features large tiled surfaces and wooden lanes, which react differently to your cleaning agents.

1. **The Power Washer:** Essential for the large blood splatters on the bowling lanes.
2. **The Mop:** Better for the kitchen and arcade areas where precision is needed around delicate machinery.
3. **Sponge:** Best for wiping down the arcade cabinets and the bowling ball return machines.
4. **Ozone Machine:** Highly recommended for the party room to quickly clear out the "smell" metric.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough: Cleaning the Facility

### Phase 1: The Kitchen and Arcade
Upon entering the building, your first stop should be the kitchen located behind the main counter. This area is cramped and filled with small physics objects that can make a mess if you are clumsy. Clear the trash into bags immediately to create a path. Once the kitchen is clear, move into the arcade. The arcade cabinets are high-value items; ensure you use the sponge to avoid water damage penalties.

### Phase 2: The Party Room and Vents
The party room is a central hub for this mission. You will find significant blood pooling near the bathroom. This is also where the mission's verticality comes into play. You will need to climb through the ventilation system accessed via the bathroom to reach the restricted bowling lane areas.

### Phase 3: The Bowling Lanes and Maintenance
Once you drop from the vents into the lanes, the scale of the cleanup increases. Use your power washer to sweep blood toward the gutters for easier collection. The maintenance room behind the pins contains the heavy machinery and is often where the final pieces of evidence are tucked away behind gears.

## All CD Collectible Locations in Town Too Small

For players aiming for the "We Are the Sound" achievement, finding the four CDs in this level is mandatory. These items are often tucked away in secret areas or behind environmental puzzles.

| CD Number | Location | Specific Instructions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **CD #2** | **Party Room Bathroom** | Located directly on the floor inside the bathroom of the VIP party room. |

### Detailed Guide for CD #4 (The Handlebar Puzzle)
The final CD in the **crime scene cleaner town too small walkthrough guide** is the most complex to retrieve. You must first navigate to the very end of the bowling alley into the maintenance room. 

1. Turn left and then left again to get behind the actual alley machinery.
2. Locate the **handlebar** sitting on one of the maintenance worktables.
3. Exit the room and cross to the completely opposite side of the bowling hall.
4. In a dark, shadowed corner, you will find a door missing its handle.
5. Interact with the door to attach the handlebar.
6. Inside, the CD is sitting on a table in the back-left corner.

> **Tip:** You do not need to finish the cleaning objectives to save your collectible progress. If you are only playing for achievements, you can pick up the CDs and exit the level immediately.

## Advanced Cleaning Tips for 100% Completion

To achieve a Perfect Rank in 2026, you must go beyond just mopping. The "Town Too Small" level has specific "hidden grime" spots that often prevent players from hitting 100%.

*   **Check the Pinsetters:** Blood often splatters onto the mechanical arms that pick up the bowling pins. You must jump up into the machinery to clean these spots.
*   **Under the Arcade Machines:** Use your flashlight (default 'F') to look under the gap between the arcade cabinets and the floor.
*   **The Ceiling Fans:** In the party room, blood can occasionally reach the ceiling fans. Use the ladder found in the maintenance closet to reach them.
*   **Ball Return Tunnels:** Don't forget to spray inside the holes where the bowling balls emerge.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the last CD in the Town Too Small level?**

A: The final CD is located in a secret room. You must find a handlebar in the maintenance area behind the bowling lanes and use it on a handle-less door in the far dark corner of the main hall.

**Q: Do I have to complete the whole mission to keep the collectibles?**

A: No. As of the 2026 updates, once you pick up a CD collectible, it is permanently saved to your profile. You can abandon the level immediately if you are only hunting for achievements.

**Q: How do I get into the vents for the crime scene cleaner town too small walkthrough guide?**

A: Go to the bathroom inside the party room. There is a stack of crates or a small stool you can use to reach the ceiling vent. Crawling through this vent is the only way to access the restricted area behind the bowling lanes.

**Q: Why is my cleaning percentage stuck at 99%?**

A: In Town Too Small, the most common missed spots are the undersides of the arcade machines and the mechanical parts of the pinsetters at the end of the bowling lanes. Use your UV light to highlight lingering traces.
