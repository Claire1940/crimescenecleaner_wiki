## Secluded Retreat: Peaks Hotel Secrets

The "Secluded Retreat" mission takes you to the snowy mountains, where the Peaks Hotel serves as a front for some truly depraved activities. This level is notorious for its automated security systems that have gone rogue, leaving a trail of both human and machine wreckage.

### Finding the Music CDs
There are multiple Music CDs hidden throughout the hotel. To find them, you must explore rooms that aren't necessarily part of your primary cleaning path. Check the nightstands in the guest suites and the shelving units in the staff-only maintenance areas.

### The Security Room and Lars's Office
To complete the level perfectly, you must access the security room to wipe the footage. This room is hidden behind a keypad-locked door near the main lobby. Once inside, you can also find a secret ledger that links the hotel’s "kinky" activities to Senator Holden. Lars’s office is located on the upper floor; look for the window he supposedly escaped through to find a hidden stash of cash tucked under the floorboards.

"Amber Alert" is perhaps the most emotionally charged mission in Act 2. It reveals the connection between the mobster's daughter and the human trafficking ring run by Dave. The cleanup here is massive, spanning across a boxing gym, a locker room, and a hidden sub-level.

Behind the lockers in the "losers" changing room, there is a loose panel. Interacting with it reveals a hidden corridor leading to the trafficking hub. This area contains the most important lore items in the act, including a recorded conversation between Dave and Senator Holden.

## Complete Crime Scene Cleaner Act 2 All Secrets Guide: Pro Tips

To achieve a perfect rating in Act 2, you must go beyond just mopping. The game rewards thoroughness and the ability to find "unlisted" objectives.

1.  **Check the Vents:** Many secret rooms in Act 2 are connected by ventilation shafts. If you find a screwdriver, keep it on you—it’s the key to several hidden caches of money.
2.  **Body Disposal Efficiency:** In levels like "Secluded Retreat," use the industrial incinerators or the woodchipper found in the maintenance shed. Disposing of bodies in these unique ways often triggers hidden achievements.
3.  **The Senator's Trail:** Keep an eye out for items marked with the "Holden" crest. Collecting these doesn't just add to your score; it unlocks the true ending of the game by providing enough leverage against the corrupt politician.
4.  **Cleaning Before Fixing:** Always clean the blood around a broken machine before attempting to fix it. If you fix the machine first, the "sparks" can sometimes obscure small blood droplets, making it impossible to hit 100%.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the hardest secret to find in the crime scene cleaner act 2 all secrets guide?**

A: Most players struggle with the Music CD in the "Secluded Retreat" mission. It is hidden behind a breakable mirror in the honeymoon suite. You must break the glass to find the small alcove containing the disc.

**Q: Do I need to find all secrets to unlock the next act?**

A: While you don't need 100% of the secrets to progress, finding the evidence against Senator Holden is required to unlock the "Justice" path in the late game. Without these secrets, you may be forced into the "Bad" ending.

**Q: Can I go back and find secrets I missed?**

A: Yes, you can use the mission select menu from your computer in the hub area. This allows you to replay any Act 2 mission with your upgraded tools, making it much easier to find hidden areas you might have missed on your first run in 2026.

**Q: How do I handle the killer robots in Secluded Retreat?**

A: You cannot "fight" the robots. You must use stealth and the hacking tool found in the lobby to disable them. Once disabled, they become "trash" that you must dispose of in the large bins outside.
