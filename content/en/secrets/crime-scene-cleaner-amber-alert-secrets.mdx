## Amber Alert Mission Overview

You will be moving through the Staff Area, a multi-level Bar, an Office, a Boxing Ring, and several Lounges.

## Secret 1: The Beer Barrel Puzzle

The first major secret is found in the **Staff Area Kitchen**. To access this, you must first navigate through the door to the right of the main entrance and head up the stairs. In the kitchen, you will notice four pipes along the wall near a bucket point.

To trigger this secret, you need to find specific beer barrels and attach them to the pipes in the correct order. The barrels are scattered around the storage area and kitchen.

### Barrel Arrangement Order (Left to Right):
1.  **Elder Gnome Stout** (Look for the barrel with the gnome label).
2.  **Monastern Trappist**
3.  **Iced Dwarf Pale Ale**
4.  **Iced Dwarf Pilsner**

Once these are placed correctly, a hidden compartment will open, rewarding you with a secret CD. This is one of the more easily missed **crime scene cleaner amber alert secrets** if you aren't paying attention to the labels on the barrels.

## Secret 2: The Dartboard & The Turtle Bucket

The most iconic of the **crime scene cleaner amber alert secrets** is located in the main Bar area. Near the football table, you will find a dartboard. To unlock the path to "Dave's Lair," you must interact with the dartboard and **score exactly 301 points**.

### How to Access the Sewers:
*   **The Puzzle:** Score 301 on the darts game.
*   **The Reward:** The wall behind the dartboard will lift, revealing a ladder.
*   **The Lair:** Descend into the sewers. Follow the trail of pizza boxes (a clear Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles reference).
*   **The Item:** Pick up the **Turtle Bucket** skin. This is a permanent equipment upgrade that changes the appearance of your bucket.

While in Dave's Lair, make sure to grab the valuable Dragon Statue and the recording on the table to ensure you are maximizing your payout for the mission.

## Secret 3: The Ghostly Painting

As you progress toward the **Maintenance Room** via the Lounges, you will find a hallway decorated with stone ruins and statues. This area contains a classic "hidden in plain sight" secret.

Look for a large painting on the wall near the stone ruins. You can actually **walk directly through the painting**. Behind it lies a secret room containing:
*   A collectible CD.
*   Three valuable Cat Statues.
*   Extra cash bundles.

This room is vital for players looking to complete the "Steal all valuables" objective, as the cat statues are worth a significant amount of money.

## Office Safe Code and Evidence

The Office is a small but dense area. To get 100% completion, you must open the locked safe and gather the "Fixed Fights" documents.

| Item | Location | Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Safe Code** | Under the Desk | **1369** |
| **VIP Keycard** | Bar 2nd Floor | Underneath Body #4 |
| **Fixed Fights** | Inside Safe | Primary Evidence |
| **Winning Scratchcards**| Desk Drawer | Middle drawer of the main desk |

The **VIP Keycard** is the most important item in the level, as it allows you to open the locked double glass doors at the entrance and provides access to the Boxing Ring. If you can't find it, check the floor carefully where you picked up the body on the bar's upper balcony.

## Secret 4: Dave's Karaoke Room

To unlock the final secret, you must first reach the **Maintenance Room**. Underneath the stairs leading down from the Maintenance Room, you will find a **Greek Statue Head**. 

Take this head back to the hallway near the Boxing Ring locker rooms. There is a headless statue socket waiting for it. Placing the head on the socket opens a hidden door to "Dave's Karaoke." 

Inside this room, you will find:
*   The **Akinohara - Matiku** cassette tape.
*   A statue smashed into a TV screen (valuable).
*   Multiple bundles of cash hidden in the drawers.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Boxing Ring itself is electrified or caged off initially. You must use the control panel in the Maintenance Room to lower the cage before you can clean the blood and retrieve the Katana evidence inside the ring.

## All Cassette Locations in Amber Alert

Finding all music tracks is a core part of the **crime scene cleaner amber alert secrets** checklist. There are four total cassettes to find in this mission:

1.  **Garbage Truck Lullaby:** Reward for completing the Beer Barrel puzzle in the Staff Area.
2.  **Dave Brawl - Entrance Theme:** Found in the Winner's Changing Room (the luxurious one) on a bench.
3.  **Lulu Suicide - Bye:** Located in the secret room behind the painting in the Lounge/Maintenance hallway.
4.  **Akinohara - Matiku:** Found inside the secret Karaoke Room (requires the Greek Statue Head).

## FAQ

**Q: What is the code for the safe in the Amber Alert mission?**

A: The code for the office safe is **1369**. You can find this written on the underside of the office desk if you crouch down and look up.

**Q: Where is the VIP Keycard located?**

A: The VIP Keycard is found on the second floor of the Bar. It is usually hidden directly underneath Body #4, who is lying on the floor near the railing. You must move the body to see the card.

**Q: How do I get the Turtle Bucket in Crime Scene Cleaner?**

A: To get the Turtle Bucket, you must solve the dartboard puzzle by scoring exactly 301. This opens a secret path to the sewers where the bucket skin is located near a pile of pizza boxes.

**Q: I'm missing one piece of furniture in the Bar, where is it?**

A: There are two commonly missed pieces: one is a bar stool located inside the Women's restroom, and another is often stuck on top of the overhead ventilation ducts. Use your power washer to knock it down. One chair from the Boxing Ring also belongs in the Bar area!
