## Mission Overview: The Haunted Manor

The Short Circuit mission (Chapter 4) takes place in a sprawling modern estate. The primary objective is to clean up the remains of a family who went mad believing their house was possessed. Before you start scrubbing the blood off the ceiling, you should focus on gathering the evidence and unlocking the restricted areas.

## Crime Scene Cleaner Short Circuit Secrets: Hidden Rooms

There are two primary **crime scene cleaner short circuit secrets** that count toward your "Secrets Found" stat. Both contain significant loot and environmental storytelling.

### Secret #1: The Hidden Gallery
To find the first secret, head to the upstairs hallway. Near the boxes where you found the fourth smart speaker, look at the wall lights (sconces). One of the sconces is tilted at an odd angle. Interact with this crooked light to trigger a mechanical door. This opens a small room containing a valuable painting and several stacks of cash.

### Secret #2: The Cupboard Under the Stairs (Easter Egg)
The second secret is a direct reference to Harry Potter. Once you have the Living Room Key, enter the living room and head toward the kitchen area near the refrigerator. Behind the fridge, you will see a very small, inconspicuous door built into the underside of the staircase. Open it and crawl inside to discover "Harry the Potter and the Goblet of Clay" and a collection of valuable figurines.

| Secret Room | Activation Method | Main Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hidden Gallery** | Interact with crooked wall sconce upstairs | Painting & Large Cash Stacks |
| **The Cupboard** | Open small door behind the kitchen fridge | 3x Figurines ($900 total) |

## How to Find Both Cassette Tapes

The cassette tapes in Short Circuit are unique because they involve the "haunted" mechanics of the house. Follow these specific steps to ensure the second one spawns correctly.

1.  **Cassette #1:** Go to the upstairs workshop (the room with the lockers and the Living Room Key). The first tape is sitting plainly on a shelf on the left wall.
2.  **Triggering Cassette #2:** To make the second tape appear, you must "experience" the haunting. After picking up the first tape, walk through every room in the house and interact with the locked doors. You may hear creepy music or see objects move. 
3.  **The Respawn:** Return to the exact same shelf where you found the first cassette. Because the house is "haunted," a second cassette will have manifested in the same spot.

> **Warning:** Some players report that the second cassette won't spawn until the "cleaning" progress is at least 25% or until the robot vacuum has been destroyed. If it isn't there, keep cleaning and check back later.

## Final Cleaning Checklist

To get a 100% score, you must do more than just find the **crime scene cleaner short circuit secrets**. This level is notorious for "hidden" dirt.

1.  **The Robot Vacuum:** There is a rogue robot vacuum wandering the house. It will smear blood everywhere if you don't stop it. Use your mop or a heavy object to destroy it early in the mission.
2.  **Paw Prints:** Look for glowing green paw prints on the floors and walls. These count as "dirt" and must be cleaned.
3.  **Ceiling Splatters:** Because the "haunting" involved high-pressure blood traps, always check the ceilings in the lounge and the living room.
4.  **Furniture Reset:** Ensure all 29 pieces of furniture (21 downstairs, 8 upstairs) are returned to their highlighted silhouettes.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the Living Room Key in the Short Circuit mission?**

A: The key is located on the second floor. Go up the stairs, turn right, and enter the workshop at the end of the hall. The key is sitting on the table next to a computer.

**Q: I found all the evidence, but why am I stuck at 99%?**

A: You likely missed the paw prints or the blood on the ceiling. Use your UV light (Sense) to check high surfaces. Also, make sure you have destroyed the robot vacuum, as it can sometimes hide under furniture.

**Q: How do I open the crime scene cleaner short circuit secrets room upstairs?**

A: Look for a wall light (sconce) that is crooked. It is located in the hallway near the boxes. Interacting with this light will slide a wall panel open to reveal the secret room.

**Q: Is there a specific order to collecting the cassettes?**

A: Yes. You must collect the first cassette from the workshop shelf first. Then, you must explore the house to trigger the "haunting" sounds before the second cassette will spawn on that same shelf.
