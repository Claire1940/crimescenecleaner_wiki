## Crime Scene Cleaner Town Too Small Secrets: Hidden Rooms

There are two primary secret rooms in this mission, plus a hidden ceiling area that contains valuable loot. Finding these is mandatory for players aiming for 100% completion and all achievements.

### 1. The Gardener’s Secret Room (Back Alleys)
This secret is located outside the main building but is best accessed through the Kitchen. 
*   **How to find it:** Go to the Kitchen and locate the metal door blocked by trash bags and boxes. Clear the debris and head outside.
*   **The Entrance:** Turn left and look for a floor-level vent on the building's exterior wall. 
*   **The Reward:** Break the vent to enter a small room. Inside, you will find a recording from the gardener and the **Jamanas FT. Michael Jabatti – Della Natura** cassette.

### 2. The Cultist Secret Room (Maintenance)
Tucked behind the bowling alley's machinery is a room that requires a specific item to enter.
*   **How to find it:** Enter the Maintenance Room located at the end of the bowling lanes. Look for a brown wooden door behind several boxes. This door is missing its handle.
*   **Finding the Handle:** Go further into the maintenance area toward the workbench near the lane controls. The **White Door Handle** is sitting on the table next to a lottery ticket.
*   **The Reward:** Attach the handle to the door to reveal a bizarre room featuring a salt circle and a rubber duck. You can pick up the **Party for Kids – Wawa Weewo** cassette here.

### 3. The Ceiling Coffin
This isn't a "room" in the traditional sense, but it is a major secret area.
*   **How to find it:** Go to the restrooms in the Public Space. In one of the cubicles, you will find a rope ladder leading into the ceiling.
*   **The Reward:** Crawl through the rafters to find a flashlight illuminating a coffin. Open it to find a mannequin (which must be destroyed and bagged) and a significant stash of cash.

## Navigating the Bowling Alley and Office

To progress through the story and find the final **crime scene cleaner town too small secrets**, you must gain access to the locked Office. This requires the **Rusty Key**, which is easily missed if you aren't looking at the floor.

### Finding the Rusty Key
The key is located in the Bowling Alley area. Look for Big Jim's body (the one with a katana in it) lying on the lane. The Rusty Key is sitting on the floor immediately next to him. Once you have it, return to the Kitchen and unlock the wooden door with the square window to enter the Office.

### Cleaning the Office
The Office contains several high-value items and critical evidence:
1.  **The Safe:** Use your skills to open the safe for a large cash stash.
2.  **The Postcard:** Look at the blue sofa. There is a white cushion; move it to find a hidden postcard (evidence).
3.  **The Blackmail Files:** These are the primary objective items located on the desk.

## Furniture and Trash: The Final Stretch

The "Town Too Small" mission is notorious for having "ghost" trash—items that show up on your cleaner sense but are hard to reach. 

*   **Wall Weapons:** Check the walls in the Arcade and Public Space. There are often throwing stars and small blades embedded high up that count as trash.
*   **Chair Management:** This mission requires you to move a lot of chairs between rooms. 
    *   **Blue/Yellow Chairs:** These belong in the Public Space/Restaurant area. You will find them scattered in the Kitchen, the Restrooms, and even outside in the Back Alleys.
    *   **Wooden Chairs:** These belong in the Party Room. Check the Kitchen and the Restroom corridor to find the missing ones.
*   **The "Let's Rock" Wall:** In the Party Room, ensure you clean the graffiti on the wall. Even if the floor is spotless, the mission won't hit 100% if the blood-writing remains.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the fourth cassette tape in the Town Too Small mission?**

A: The four cassettes are located in the Party Room (behind a victim), the Bowling Alley (on the counter), the Secret Vent Room in the Back Alleys, and the Secret Handle Room in the Maintenance area.

**Q: I can't find the last piece of trash in the Public Space. Where could it be?**

A: Check the walls very carefully. Small throwing stars (shurikens) are often stuck in the wooden paneling or high up near the ceiling. Use your cleaner sense to highlight them in yellow.

**Q: How do I get into the Bowling Alley if the double doors are locked?**

A: You cannot open the double doors from the restaurant side initially. You must go to the restrooms, climb the rope ladder in the cubicle, navigate the ceiling, and drop down into the bowling alley from above. You can then break the boards on the double doors to create a shortcut.

**Q: What do I do with the mannequin pieces in the ceiling?**

A: Even though they look like props, the mannequin pieces in the coffin count as trash. You must destroy the mannequin, pick up the pieces, and bag them for your truck to achieve a 100% score and uncover all **crime scene cleaner town too small secrets**.
