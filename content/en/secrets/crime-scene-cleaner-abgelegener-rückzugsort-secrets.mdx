## Navigating the Security Protocols

The "Man versus Machine" theme is prevalent throughout this level. When you first arrive, Amber (your hacker contact) will inform you that the security protocols have been triggered. This opens all doors but also activates hazardous defense systems.

1.  **Locate the Security Room:** This should be your first priority. Disabling the primary threat makes the cleanup significantly easier.
2.  **Handle the Androids:** Unlike human bodies, android parts can be heavy and may require multiple trips to the disposal bin. They often leak synthetic fluids that require specific detergents.
3.  **Check the Mirrors:** The protagonist notes a dislike for mirrors in this level, hinting that they are "too reflective." In gaming terms, this often means hidden rooms or cameras are behind them.

> **Tip:** Use your UV light extensively in the suites. The "fun" the rich people had here leaves traces that aren't always visible to the naked eye.

## Advanced Cleaning Techniques for Secluded Retreat

Because of the snowy environment and the high-tech nature of the Peaks Hotel, your standard mop might not be enough. The bloodstains here are often mixed with hydraulic fluid and expensive champagne, creating a stubborn residue.

*   **The "Reflective" Problem:** When cleaning mirrors, ensure you use the squeegee tool to prevent streaks, which can count against your "Perfect Cleanup" rating.
