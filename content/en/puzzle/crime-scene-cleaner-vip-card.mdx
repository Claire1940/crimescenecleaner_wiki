In the gritty world of **Crime Scene Cleaner**, playing as Kovalsky means more than just mopping up blood and disposing of bodies for the mob. To truly excel and earn the maximum score, you must uncover every hidden detail and secret area tucked away in the game's complex levels. One of the most common hurdles players face is locating the **crime scene cleaner vip card** during the "Trial by Blood" mission. This mission takes you to Miko's Spa & Wellness Center, a neon-soaked environment where the evidence is plentiful but the secrets are guarded behind a complex web of locker keys and security panels.

Finding the **crime scene cleaner vip card** is essential for unlocking the "Gold on the Ceiling" achievement and accessing the lucrative VIP Zone. This high-end area contains significant cash piles, valuable statues to steal, and the final clues needed to reach 100% completion. In this guide, we will walk you through the exact sequence of events required to secure the VIP card and navigate the spa's hidden compartments.

## The Locker Key Chain: Step-by-Step

The "Trial by Blood" mission relies on a "chain" of keys. You cannot simply walk up to the VIP locker; you must find specific cards in a predetermined order to progress. Most of these cards are found within lockers in the Men's and Women's Locker Rooms or scattered in nearby relaxation areas.

Follow this specific sequence to navigate the locker puzzle:

| Step | Action | Location | Key/Item Obtained |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Locate the card on the table | **Tanning Room** (Right of Relaxation Zone) | **W024 Locker Card** |
| **2** | Open Locker W024 | **Women's Locker Room** | **M034 Locker Card** |
| **3** | Open Locker M034 | **Men's Locker Room** | **VIP Card** |
| **4** | Locate the card in the pool | **Relaxation Zone** (Must drain pool first) | **M017 Locker Card** |
| **5** | Open Locker M017 | **Men's Locker Room** | **Cassette Tape #2** |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep your flashlight on when searching the locker rooms. The cards are small, pink or blue rectangles that can easily be missed against the dark floors or metallic benches.

## Accessing the Hidden Room and VIP Zone

Once you have the **crime scene cleaner vip card** in your inventory, you aren't finished yet. The card itself doesn't open a door; it must be used on a specific security panel located within a *different* hidden room.

### Step 1: The Hacking Mini-Game
Before using the VIP card, you must enter the first secret area. In the Relaxation Zone, look for a door with a red keypad. This keypad is part of a hacking mini-game. You need to click the numbers as the moving vertical line aligns with them. Once successfully hacked, the door will slide open, revealing a small office/control room.

### Step 2: Using the VIP Card
Inside this newly opened hidden room, look at the wall to the left of the computer desk. You will see a high-tech device with a card slot. Interact with it to use your **crime scene cleaner vip card**. This action triggers a mechanical sound, and a large hidden compartment will open back in the main pool area of the Relaxation Zone.

## Draining the Pool: The Number Puzzle

To find all the secrets and the M017 card, you must drain the blood-filled pool. This is often where players get stuck because the draining mechanism requires a specific input.

1.  **Find the Control Panel:** Go to the small room near the pool with the switchboard.
2.  **Input the Code:** You will see numbers like **0, 2, 9, 5** (or similar variations depending on your difficulty settings). Interact with the dials to match the sequence.
3.  **Activate the Drain:** Once the numbers are set, the pool will begin to empty.
4.  **Collect the Loot:** At the bottom of the empty pool, near the drain, you will find the **M017 Locker Card**. This is vital for collecting the second cassette tape.

Cassette tapes are the primary collectibles in **Crime Scene Cleaner**. Finding all three in this mission is required for a perfect rating.

| Tape # | Method of Discovery | Exact Location |

## Unlocking the Final Secret: Locker W018

The final secret in the "Trial by Blood" mission is hidden behind one last locker. Many players forget to check the VIP Zone for further items once they've cleared the cash and statues.

Inside the VIP Zone (the room opened by the **crime scene cleaner vip card**), look on the table. You will find the **W018 Locker Card**. Take this card back to the Women's Locker Room. Opening Locker 18 will reveal the second major secret of the level and contribute to your 100% completion stat.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not end the mission until you have confirmed that both "Secrets" and all three "Cassettes" are checked off in your objective list. Once you leave via the elevator, you cannot return without restarting the entire level.

## Cleaning for Perfection

While hunting for the **crime scene cleaner vip card**, don't forget your primary objective. The spa is filled with intricate tiles and glass surfaces that show blood smears easily under UV light. 

*   **UV Lamp is King:** Use the UV lamp constantly in the locker rooms. Blood often splatters on the top of the lockers or inside the open ones.
*   **Body Disposal:** Ensure all bodies are bagged and placed in the designated disposal area before you spend too much time on the VIP puzzles.
*   **Evidence Collection:** Several pieces of evidence (like incriminating phones or weapons) are located inside the lockers you unlock while searching for the VIP card.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I find the W024 card in the Tanning Room?**

A: The card is usually sitting on a small side table or bench near the tanning beds. If it's not there, check the floor nearby; physics objects can sometimes be knocked around if you've been using the power washer or bumping into furniture.

**Q: Does the VIP card carry over between missions?**

A: No. The **crime scene cleaner vip card** is a mission-specific item for "Trial by Blood." Each mission has its own unique keys and access cards that must be found within that specific level.

**Q: How do I get the "Gold on the Ceiling" achievement?**

A: This achievement is unlocked the moment you use the VIP card on the panel inside the hidden room and successfully trigger the opening of the VIP Zone.

**Q: I opened Locker M034 but the VIP card wasn't there. What happened?**

A: Ensure you are in the correct locker room. There are two separate areas: the Men's Locker Room and the Women's Locker Room. M-series keys always belong to the Men's room, and W-series to the Women's. If the locker is empty, you may have already picked it up without noticing—check your inventory/keyring.
