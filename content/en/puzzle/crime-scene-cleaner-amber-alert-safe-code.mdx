## Finding the Amber Alert Safe Code

The safe is located in the **Office** area on the upper floor of the bar. To reach it, you can either head through the restroom corridor or break down the damaged door on the second floor of the bar. Once inside, you will find a locked safe that requires a four-digit combination.

The **crime scene cleaner amber alert safe code is 1369**. If you want to find it yourself in-game, crouch and look directly **underneath the main office desk**. The numbers are scribbled on the underside of the wood. When interacting with the safe, you must click or type the numbers as the slider passes over them on the dial.

## Unlocking Secret Areas

To achieve a perfect score in 2026, players must uncover the three hidden secrets within the Amber Alert level. These secrets often contain unique collectibles like cassette tapes or custom bucket skins.

### 1. The Sewer Lair (Turtle Bucket)
In the main bar area, locate the dartboard. To open the secret passage, you must **score exactly 301** on the dartboard. This reveals a ladder leading down to "Dave's Lair" in the sewers. Here, you can find the **Turtle Bucket skin**, a nod to famous pizza-eating ninjas.

### 2. The Beer Barrel Puzzle
In the Staff Area kitchen, there is an elevator alcove. You must find the specific beer barrels and attach them to the pipes in a specific order based on the draft taps in the bar.
*   **Correct Order:** Elder Gnome Stout → Monastern Trappist → Iced Dwarf Pale Ale → Iced Dwarf Pilsner.
*   **Reward:** "Garbage Truck Lullaby" Cassette.

### 3. The Painting Room
On the Lounges level, head toward the Maintenance Room entrance. There is a wall decorated with stone ruins and a large painting. You can actually **walk directly through the painting** to find a hidden room containing valuable cat statues and a secret CD.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some furniture pieces are extremely well hidden. One chair is located on top of the overhead vents in the bar—you will need to use the power washer to knock it down.

## Efficient Cleaning Strategy

Because the Amber Alert mission is so vast, managing your water and trash is essential. Start by setting up your bucket at the water tap in the **Entrance** area, then move to the **Staff Area** kitchen tap as you progress deeper into the club.

1.  **Clear the Bodies First:** Use your bin or carry them to the van immediately to clear floor space.
2.  **Use the Power Washer for Ceilings:** High-end clubs have high ceilings. Blood splatter often reaches the rafters in the Boxing Ring area.
3.  **The Maintenance Room Loop:** You must enter the Maintenance Room to lower the cage around the boxing ring. Without doing this, you cannot clean the blood inside the ring or collect the Katana evidence.
4.  **Final Sweep:** Use your "Cleaner Sense" (V key by default) to highlight tiny droplets of blood or small pieces of trash like cigarette butts that are easily missed in the dark wood textures of the bar.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the crime scene cleaner amber alert safe code?**

A: The code is **1369**. You can find it written on the underside of the desk in the main office.

**Q: How do I open the Boxing Ring cage?**

A: You must find the Maintenance Room, which is accessed through the Lounges. Inside, there is a control panel near the window that allows you to lower the cage.

**Q: I am missing one piece of furniture in the Bar area. Where is it?**

A: Check the overhead vents. One chair is often stuck on top of the ventilation ducting. Additionally, there is a wooden chair in the Boxing Ring that actually belongs to the Bar area.

**Q: Where is the VIP Keycard located?**

A: The VIP Keycard is on the second floor of the bar. It is usually hidden underneath or very close to Body #4, near the entrance to the office corridor.
