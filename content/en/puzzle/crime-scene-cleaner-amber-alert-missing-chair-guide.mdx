## The Mystery of the Missing Bar Chair

The Bar area in Amber Alert requires you to return a massive amount of furniture—usually 32 to 34 pieces depending on your current game version. Most players easily find the stools and tables scattered around the main floor, but two specific items are consistently missed.

The first "missing" chair is actually located **inside the Boxing Ring**. Even though you are cleaning the Bar checklist, one of the wooden chairs that belongs to the bar is currently being used as a seat for the dead boxer in the center of the ring. You must lower the cage in the Maintenance Room to access the ring, then carry that chair all the way back to the main bar area.

The second commonly missed item is located on top of the **overhead ventilation ducts**. During the struggle, furniture can sometimes be clipped or thrown upward. Use your power washer to spray the top of the vents; if a chair is stuck there, the water pressure will knock it down so you can place it correctly.

## Step-by-Step Area Walkthrough

To ensure you don't miss the items mentioned in our **crime scene cleaner amber alert missing chair guide**, it is best to tackle the building in a specific order.

### 1. Staff Area and Kitchen
Start here to get your bucket filled. There is a body on the stairs and a valuable ring in the lockers. 
*   **The Beer Barrel Secret**: In the kitchen, find the barrel with the "Elder Gnome Stout" label. Ride the small elevator down and arrange the barrels to match the taps in the bar (Elder Gnome, Monastern, Iced Dwarf Pale Ale, Iced Dwarf Pilsner). This unlocks a secret CD.

### 2. The Bar and the Dartboard Secret
This is where the bulk of the furniture resides. Clean the blood first so you don't slip while carrying chairs.
*   **The Dartboard Secret**: Head to the back of the bar near the football table. You need to score exactly **301** on the dartboard. Doing so opens a passage to "Dave's Lair" in the sewers.
*   **Sewer Rewards**: Down here, you'll find the **Turtle Bucket** skin and a recording. This area is essential for the "Ninja Turtle" inspired achievement.

### 3. The Office (Safe Code: 1369)
The Office is located upstairs. The door is often damaged, so you may need to bash it open.
*   **The Safe**: Look under the desk to find the code **1369**. Inside the safe, you'll find evidence of fixed fights and several bundles of cash.
*   **The VIP Card**: You cannot enter the Boxing Ring without the VIP Card. It is located on the second floor of the bar, hidden underneath **Body #4**.

### 4. The Boxing Ring and Maintenance Room
Use the VIP Card to enter. The ring is caged off initially.
*   **Maintenance Room**: Go to the Lounges and find the door with the snake skeleton. Inside the Maintenance Room, use the control panel to lower the boxing ring cage. 
*   **The Missing Chair**: Once the cage is down, grab the wooden chair the boxer was sitting on. **This belongs to the Bar area**, not the ring. Carry it back to the bar to complete your checklist.

## Secret Locations and Collectibles

Beyond the furniture, Amber Alert contains three major secrets that provide unique rewards and CDs.

1.  **The Sewer Lair**: Accessed via the Dartboard puzzle in the Bar. Contains the Turtle Bucket.
2.  **The Beer Cellar**: Accessed via the elevator in the Staff Area kitchen. Requires the barrel-ordering puzzle.
3.  **The Hidden Karaoke Room**: In the Boxing Ring area, find the Greek head statue at the bottom of the Maintenance Room stairs. Place it on the socket near the Winner’s locker room to open "Dave's Karaoke."

> **Warning:** Be careful when throwing trash bags near the secrets. If a bag falls into an inaccessible area of the sewers, you may be unable to reach 100% cleanliness.
