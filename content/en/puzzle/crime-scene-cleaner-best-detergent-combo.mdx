## Understanding Detergent Types and Effects

Before you can master the **crime scene cleaner best detergent combo**, you must understand the individual components available in your arsenal. Detergents are not just passive buffs; they fundamentally alter the physics of how your tools interact with grime and blood. You activate these effects by pouring the detergent into a filled water bucket and then rinsing your mop or sponge. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you accidentally spill your bucket or decide to replace the water, the detergent effect is immediately lost. Always finish a room or a large section before refreshing your water supply to avoid wasting expensive chemicals.

| Detergent Name | Primary Effect | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Resistance** | Tools get dirty slower | Large rooms with light blood splatters |
| **Clean** | Water stays clean longer | High-density gore areas requiring frequent rinsing |
| **Power** | Cleans tough stains faster | Dried blood and "Red" UV-visible stains |
| **Area** | Increases cleaning radius | Ceilings, large floors, and open warehouses |
| **Cleaner** (Hybrid) | Resistance + Clean | Mid-game general maintenance |
| **Pure** (Hybrid) | Area + Power | Late-game speedrunning and efficiency |

2.  **Detergent Efficiency:** Increases the number of uses you get out of a single bottle before needing to restock.
3.  **Various Detergent Bonus:** A late-game perk that provides a cumulative efficiency boost when you mix different types of chemicals.

*   **Best Tool:** Dual-wielded Sponges

*   **Benefit:** Eliminates the need for multiple passes on tough stains.

## Tool-Specific Detergent Strategies

While the mop is your primary tool, the **crime scene cleaner best detergent combo** should change when you switch to specialized equipment. For instance, the power washer—unlocked early but heavily upgradable—interacts differently with chemicals than the sponge does.

### The Power Washer Setup
For the power washer, size and power are everything. Many players have found success using **1x Power Detergent and 2x Area/Size Detergents**. Because the power washer already cleans fairly quickly, increasing the "splash zone" of the water jet allows you to spray down entire hallways in seconds.

### The Dual-Sponge Technique
Once you reach the final tiers of the sponge upgrade, you will be dual-wielding them. This tool is best for vertical surfaces and furniture. The best combo here is **Pure Detergent + Resistance**. This ensures that as you "wax on, wax off" across the walls, you aren't leaving streaks or requiring constant trips to the bucket.

> **💡 Tip:** Always use your "Cleaner Sense" (Q key) after applying a detergent combo. The visual outlines will help you see exactly how much more surface area you are covering with your new buffs.

## Advanced Tips for Professional Cleaning

To truly master the **crime scene cleaner best detergent combo**, you need to integrate it into a broader workflow. Cleaning is only part of the job; you also have bodies to dispose of, evidence to collect, and furniture to restore.

*   **UV Light Synergy:** Always scan a room with the UV light before choosing your detergent. If the room is glowing bright red, prioritize **Power**. If it is mostly light splatters across a large area, prioritize **Area**.
*   **The "Unlimited" Perk:** Aim for the "Unlimited Detergent Use" skill as early as possible. This removes the resource management aspect of chemicals, allowing you to experiment with different mixes without worrying about the cost.
*   **Furniture Restoration:** Remember that you must put furniture back in its place to finish a level. Using an **Area** detergent buffed mop around furniture can sometimes clean the floor *under* the objects without you having to move every single chair.

## FAQ

**Q: Is detergent actually necessary to finish the game?**

A: No, you can technically finish every mission using only plain water. However, it will take significantly longer, especially in late-game missions like the Luxury Mansion or the Art Museum. Using the **crime scene cleaner best detergent combo** is highly recommended for anyone looking to 100% the game or earn the "S" rank on missions.

**Q: Can I mix more than three detergents?**

A: Currently, the game limits you to three detergent slots in a standard bucket. However, by using hybrid detergents like **Pure** or **Cleaner**, you can effectively gain the benefits of four different effects simultaneously.

**Q: Does the detergent affect the power washer?**

A: Yes. When you fill your power washer from a bucket that contains a detergent mix, the power washer inherits those buffs. This is essential for the late-game levels where the power washer becomes your primary cleaning tool.

**Q: What is the best detergent combo for the "Red Stains" under UV light?**

A: The most effective way to handle red stains is to stack **Power Detergent** with **Pure Detergent**. This provides the maximum cleaning strength available in the game, allowing you to erase even the most stubborn dried blood in a single pass.
