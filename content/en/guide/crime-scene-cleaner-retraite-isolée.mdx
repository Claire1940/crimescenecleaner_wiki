## Mission Overview: The White Peaks Hotel

The Secluded Retreat takes place in a remote, snowy mountain range where the wealthy go to indulge in "nightmare fuel" entertainment. The mission begins with your handler, Amber, informing you that the hotel's security protocols have been triggered, making your job as Mr. Kowalski significantly more difficult. You aren't just cleaning up after people; you are cleaning up after a "Man versus Machine" catastrophe where the machines clearly won.

The layout of the hotel is sprawling, featuring multiple floors, a security room, a massage parlor, and the private office of the elusive Lars. Because the area is so isolated, you have the "peace of mind" that no one is coming to help, but that also means the scale of the mess is entirely on your shoulders.

## Navigating the Secluded Retreat

When you first arrive at the **crime scene cleaner retraite isolée**, the atmosphere is deceptively cozy. However, once you step inside, the "run to the core" nature of the establishment becomes apparent. Your first major hurdle is the security system. You cannot fully clean the hotel if the doors remain locked.

> **💡 Tip:** Head to the security room early. Hacking the terminal here will trigger a "The eliminated" announcement, which unlocks all doors and allows you to move freely between the massage rooms and the private suites.

The "Man versus Machine" theme isn't just for show. You will encounter various "tin cans" or androids that have been hacked or gone haywire. These must be treated like any other piece of evidence. Some need to be chopped, while others can be tossed directly into the industrial trash bins.

## Secrets and Cassette Locations

For the completionists playing Crime Scene Cleaner, the Secluded Retreat (retraite isolée) contains several hidden items that provide deeper lore into the world of Lars and his "godless" associates. Finding these cassettes often involves looking in places where "free roaming types" wouldn't usually look, such as behind tight cages or under discarded furniture.

1.  **The Security Room Cassette:** Located near the main terminal after you override the lockdown.
2.  **The Massage Parlor Secret:** Check the lockers in the staff area of the relaxation wing.
3.  **Lars’s Office Evidence:** While looking for the associates list, check the side drawers for a hidden cassette regarding Senator Hullman.
4.  **The Snowy Balcony:** There is a secret area accessible through a window in the upper suites that contains a hidden collectible.

> **Warning:** Be careful when cleaning near mirrors. Some players report that the reflection mechanics can make it difficult to see small blood spots unless the UV lamp is used at a specific angle.

## Advanced Cleaning Strategies

The **crime scene cleaner retraite isolée** level introduces complex verticality. You will often find yourself cleaning a ceiling or a high wall because an android exploded. 

### Dealing with Androids
Unlike human bodies, androids are bulky and "soulless." You cannot simply bag them. You must often use your chopper to break them into smaller components. These components are heavy and will slow you down. It is best to clear a path to the dumpster before you start moving the heavy metal parts.

### The Mirror Room
One of the most frustrating areas in the White Peaks Hotel is the room filled with mirrors. As Kowalski notes, they make him feel "too reflective." From a gameplay perspective, blood on mirrors is harder to see and requires the sponge rather than the mop to avoid leaving streaks. Ensure you use your UV light frequently in this section.

## The Story of Lars and Senator Hullman

As you progress through the **crime scene cleaner retraite isolée** mission, you uncover a plot involving Senator Hullman, a "guardian of traditional values" who seems to be deeply involved in the hotel's illicit activities. The dialogue between Amber and Kowalski suggests that the "role playing" at White Peaks Hotel went beyond simple entertainment, leading to the violent robot uprising you are currently cleaning up.

Finding the associates list in Lars’s office is the climax of the mission. Lars himself has escaped through a window, leaving you to deal with the fallout. This narrative beat is crucial for players following the overarching story of [Crime Scene Cleaner on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1040200/Crime_Scene_Cleaner/), as it links the local gangs to high-level political corruption.

## Efficiency Tips for 100% Completion

*   **Empty the Trash First:** Before you start mopping, clear out the large debris. It’s easy to trip over a "tin can" arm while carrying a bucket of dirty water.
*   **Check the Cages:** The tight cages in the basement area often hide small pieces of trash or blood splatters that are easy to miss.
*   **Listen to Kowalski:** The protagonist often gives verbal cues when something is "blocking the way" or if a room is particularly "nightmare fuel."
*   **The Snowy Road:** Don't forget to check the entrance area. Some "eliminated" guests may have tried to flee, leaving a trail of blood in the snow outside.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the security room in the crime scene cleaner retraite isolée mission?**

A: The security room is located on the ground floor, past the main lobby. Look for the door with the electronic keypad. Hacking this room is mandatory to open the rest of the hotel.

**Q: How do I dispose of the large robots?**

A: You must use the chopper tool to break the androids into smaller pieces. Once broken down, they can be placed in standard trash bags or carried individually to the industrial bin outside the hotel.

**Q: I’m stuck at 99% cleanliness, what am I missing?**

A: In the **crime scene cleaner retraite isolée** level, the most commonly missed spots are on the mirrors in the massage wing and small blood droplets on the snowy path leading to the hotel entrance. Use your UV lamp to scan the ceilings as well.

**Q: Who is Senator Hullman?**

A: Senator Hullman is a key antagonist mentioned in the lore of the Secluded Retreat. He is a public figure who preaches traditional values while secretly participating in the dangerous "entertainment" options provided by Lars at the White Peaks Hotel.
