## Mission Overview: The Amber Alert Contract

The "Amber Alert" mission is a significant step up in complexity compared to earlier levels. You are cleaning up after Amber, who has proven to be far more ruthless than her father anticipated. The level is split into several distinct zones: the main bar and lounge, the backstage locker rooms, the dual "rings" (boxing and trafficking), and the sewer access points.

| Zone | Primary Hazard | Key Objectives |
|---|---|---|
| **Main Bar** | Poisoned Glassware | Clean blood, dispose of bottles, scrub floors |
| **Locker Rooms** | Scattered Trash | Collect evidence, clean lockers, find cassettes |
| **Boxing Ring** | Large Blood Volume | Dispose of "Dave," clean the ring and seats |
| **Trafficking Area** | Evidence of Crimes | Remove restraints, clean torture implements |
| **Sewers** | Biohazards | Find the "Ninja" secret, clean pizza boxes |

> **Warning:** Be careful when moving through the bar area. Breaking glass bottles creates more trash and can spread blood further, increasing your total cleaning time.

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough: The Bar and Lounge

Upon entering the facility, you’ll immediately notice the "Heat" and "Poison" themes. Amber didn't just use bullets; she used the environment. Your first priority should be the main floor. 

1.  **Clear the Entrance:** Start by picking up the larger pieces of trash. In this level, there is a significant amount of "pizza" trash, a nod to the teenage mutant themes hidden later in the level.
2.  **The Bar Counter:** There are several bodies and poisoned drinks here. Make sure to use your UV light to check the undersides of the bar stools.
3.  **Body Disposal:** You will encounter several victims here. Use the designated body bags and move them to the extraction point early to clear space for mopping.

## Cleaning the Boxing and Trafficking Rings

The most gruesome part of the "Amber Alert" mission involves the two rings. Dave, the primary target, met his end in the boxing ring, while the second ring reveals a darker side of the mob's operations.

### The Boxing Ring
The boxing ring is covered in "red" and requires heavy-duty scrubbing. Use the power washer for the canvas, as the mop often leaves streaks on the fabric texture. Don't forget to check the spectators' seats; blood splatter often travels further than you expect in this engine.

### The Trafficking Ring
This area is psychologically heavy and filled with evidence. You aren't just a garbage man here; you are a "trash collector" of the mob's worst secrets. Ensure you pick up all the restraints and dispose of the evidence of human trafficking to satisfy the mission's "no witnesses" requirement.

| Item Type | Location | Action Required |
|---|---|---|
| **Dave's Body** | Center of Boxing Ring | Bag and Extract |
| **Golden Ticket** | Near the Trafficking Entrance | Collect for Secret |
| **Senator's Files** | Office Area | Collect for Blackmail/Story |
| **Ninja Pizza** | Sewer Entrance | Dispose of as Trash |

## Finding All Secrets and Cassettes

To achieve a 100% completion in your **crime scene cleaner amber alert walkthrough** run, you must find the hidden cassettes and secret rooms. This level contains a famous Easter egg referencing the Ninja Turtles, located in the sewer section.

### Secret Room: The Sewer Lair
Look for a section of the wall in the lower maintenance area that looks slightly out of place. Interacting with it reveals a room filled with pizza boxes and a "wise sensei" vibe. This is where you will find one of the high-value collectibles.

### Cassette Locations
*   **Cassette 1:** Behind the bar, tucked under the sink near the poisoned shaker.
*   **Cassette 2:** In the winners' locker room, hidden inside a locker that requires a slight nudge to open.
*   **Cassette 3:** Near the trafficking ring, located on a shelf behind some crates.

> **Tip:** Always use your "Cleaner Vision" (UV light) when entering a new room. Some secrets are only visible when the light catches the metallic sheen of the cassette tape.

## Advanced Cleaning Strategies

To master the mechanics of [Crime Scene Cleaner on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1040200/Crime_Scene_Cleaner/), you need to optimize your workflow. In the Amber Alert mission, the sheer volume of blood in the boxing ring can overwhelm your bucket quickly.

1.  **Bucket Management:** Place your bucket near the center of the room. Don't walk across clean floors with a dirty mop, or you'll leave footprints that lower your score.
2.  **Power Washer Efficiency:** Use the power washer for the walls and the ring canvas, but stick to the mop for the tiled bar areas to avoid splashing blood onto clean surfaces.
3.  **Furniture Moving:** In the locker rooms, you must move the benches. Blood often pools underneath them, and the game’s "100% Clean" requirement is very strict about these hidden spots.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get 100% cleaning score on the Amber Alert mission?**

A: You must ensure all blood is removed (check under furniture), all trash is collected (including small pizza crusts), and all bodies are disposed of. Using the UV light is essential to find microscopic blood splatters on the ceiling and walls.

**Q: Where is the "Golden Ticket" located in the Amber Alert level?**

A: The Golden Ticket is typically found in the transition area between the locker rooms and the trafficking ring. It is a small, glowing item that is easy to miss if you are rushing.

**Q: Can I fail the mission if I don't find the Senator's files?**

A: No, the files are considered an optional objective/secret for story progression. However, in this **crime scene cleaner amber alert walkthrough**, we recommend collecting them to fully understand the lore behind Amber’s rise to power.

**Q: What is the best way to clean the boxing ring canvas?**

A: The power washer is the most effective tool for the canvas. The mop is less effective on the porous material and often requires multiple passes to reach a "Perfect" state.
