## Level Overview: Toxic Love

The "Toxic Love" mission takes place in a multi-story residence that has been the site of a violent altercation. Unlike previous levels that might have been more contained, Act 3 introduces more verticality and hidden passages that can easily be missed if you are rushing. Your primary objectives involve removing bloodstains, disposing of incriminating evidence, and restoring the home to its original state.

The atmosphere is heavy with narrative environmental storytelling. As you scrub the floors, you will piece together the events of the crime. However, your focus must remain on the task at hand: efficiency. The level is divided into several key zones: the living room (featuring a prominent drum kit), a narrow corridor with a hidden secret, and the upstairs bathroom.

## Detailed Cleaning Strategy

To maximize your score in this **crime scene cleaner act 3 guide**, you should follow a systematic approach to cleaning. Start from the furthest point and work your way toward the exit, or focus on one room at a time to ensure no spot is left behind.

### 1. Initial Trash Removal
Before you start mopping, clear the area of large debris and trash. This includes broken furniture, bottles, and any items labeled as evidence. Clearing the floor space makes it much easier to see the blood patterns. Use your trash bags efficiently; don't overfill them, as they can break and create a secondary mess that adds to your workload.

### 2. Managing the Bloodstains
The living room contains a significant amount of blood near the drum kit. Use your mop for the bulk of the work, but keep a sponge handy for tight corners and furniture legs. Remember to change your water frequently. Using dirty water will leave streaks and prevent you from reaching the 100% cleanliness threshold.

> **Expert Tip:** Use your "Cleaner Sense" frequently. This highlight mode reveals lingering blood droplets that are invisible to the naked eye, especially under furniture or on darker floor textures.

### 3. Lighting and Visibility
One of the first things you should do upon entering the house is locate the light switches. Working in the dark is a recipe for missing spots. In "Toxic Love," turning on the lights upstairs is crucial for the bathroom section, as the shadows can easily hide smaller stains.

## Collectibles and Secrets

Finding all collectibles is a hallmark of a master cleaner. In Act 3, there are two cassettes and one major secret area to uncover. Following the steps below will ensure you don't leave the level with an incomplete collection.

### Cassette Locations
Cassettes provide additional lore and music for your van. In "Toxic Love," they are placed in locations that require a bit of exploration.

*   **Cassette 1:** Located in the living room area. Head toward the drum kit. Turn slightly to find a bookcase; the cassette is sitting on one of the shelves, easily accessible but easy to overlook if you're focused on the floor.
*   **Cassette 2:** Found in the upstairs bathroom. It is located on the floor in the corner of the room. Ensure you've cleared the trash in the bathroom first to make it more visible.

### The Hidden Secret
Act 3 features a hidden door that leads to a corridor covered in blood. This is a critical area for both cleaning completion and finding the level's secret.

1.  Locate the bookcase near the drum kit in the living room.
2.  Interact with the hidden door mechanism (often a specific book or a prompt on the wall).
3.  Enter the corridor and head to the very end where a chair is placed.
4.  Interact with the **Port Picture** on the wall. This action triggers the secret flag for the level.

| Collectible/Secret | Location | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Cassette #1** | Living Room | On the bookcase near the drum kit. |
| **Cassette #2** | Bathroom | On the floor in the corner. |
| **Secret Room** | Living Room Wall | Hidden door leading to a bloody corridor. |
| **Secret Trigger** | End of Corridor | The Port Picture near the chair. |

## Recommended Tools and Upgrades

By the time you reach Act 3, you should have some skill points and cash to spend on upgrades. For "Toxic Love," we recommend focusing on the following:

*   **Mop Reach:** Increasing the radius of your mop will significantly speed up the living room cleanup.
*   **Water Capacity:** Reducing the number of trips to the sink or bucket is the best way to save time.
*   **Ozone Machine:** If you have unlocked the Ozone machine, use it in the hidden corridor to eliminate lingering odors and fine particles.
*   **Flashlight Battery:** While the house has lights, a stronger flashlight helps when checking under the drum kit and behind furniture.

> **Warning:** Be careful when moving furniture. Knocking over items can break them, potentially creating more "trash" that needs to be bagged and disposed of.

## Achieving a Perfect Score

To get a 100% rating in this **crime scene cleaner act 3 guide**, you must be meticulous. The game tracks three main metrics: Cleanliness, Trash Removal, and Evidence Disposal.

1.  **Cleanliness:** Ensure the "Cleaned" bar at the top of your UI is completely full. If it’s stuck at 99%, check the ceiling or the undersides of tables.
2.  **Trash:** Every piece of paper, broken glass, and pizza box must be in a bag, and every bag must be in your van.
3.  **Evidence:** Items marked with a specific icon must be handled according to your client's instructions. In "Toxic Love," ensure all weapons and incriminating documents are removed from the premises.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the hidden door in Crime Scene Cleaner Act 3?**

A: The hidden door is located in the main living room, right near the drum kit and the bookcase where you find the first cassette. Interacting with the wall/bookcase area will reveal a bloody corridor.

**Q: How do I find the secret in the Toxic Love level?**

A: To trigger the secret in this **crime scene cleaner act 3 guide**, you must enter the hidden corridor behind the living room bookcase, walk to the end where the chair is, and interact with the Port Picture on the wall.

**Q: I'm stuck at 98% cleaning in Act 3. What am I missing?**

A: Check the legs of the drum kit and the area behind the bathroom door. Blood often splatters on the vertical surfaces of furniture or gets trapped in the door swing area. Use your Cleaner Sense to highlight small droplets.

**Q: Can I go back for the cassettes if I finish cleaning?**

A: You can collect the cassettes at any time before you interact with your van to leave the level. It is usually best to collect them early so you don't forget them in the rush to finish.
