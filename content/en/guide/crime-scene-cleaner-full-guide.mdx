## Core Gameplay Mechanics and Objectives

Every mission in 2026 follows a specific set of objectives that must be met to achieve a "Perfect" rating. Failing to find a single piece of evidence or leaving a chair out of place can drastically reduce your earnings.

### 1. Blood and Dirt Removal
The most obvious task is cleaning the blood. Use your "Cleaner Sense" (default key: Q) to highlight stains in red. This vision also shows you where furniture belongs. If you find yourself stuck at 99% blood, check the ceilings, the undersides of tables, or even behind doors you’ve recently opened.

### 2. Evidence Disposal
Criminals are messy. You will need to find and dispose of murder weapons, incriminating files, and personal items. Some evidence is hidden behind hacking puzzles. When hacking, you'll often need to match sequences or crack codes found in nearby notes or digital logs.

### 3. Body Disposal
Bodies must be bagged and carried to your truck or a designated "skip bin." Be careful when moving bodies; they are heavy and can leave new blood trails if not handled properly. In some levels, you can use environmental shortcuts—like throwing bodies out of second-story windows—to save time.

### 4. Furniture Restoration
A crime scene often looks like a war zone. You must return the environment to its "pre-crime" state. This includes standing up tipped chairs, replacing cushions, and hanging paintings. This **crime scene cleaner full guide** recommends using your sense vision frequently to see the "ghost" outlines of where objects should be placed.

## Advanced Strategies for 100% Completion

Achieving 100% in the later stages of the game is a true test of patience. In the Museum level, for example, players often struggle with the "stacked painting" bug, where two items clip into each other. If you are missing one piece of furniture, try moving items you've already placed to ensure nothing is hidden underneath.

To find all secrets and cassettes:
*   **Listen for Music:** Secrets are often located near the sound of faint music or mechanical humming.
*   **Smash Everything:** Use your axe or heavy objects to break suspicious wall panels.
*   **Check the Heights:** Developers love hiding cassettes on top of high shelves or light fixtures.
*   **Steal for Profit:** While your main goal is cleaning, stealing "fertilizer," jewelry, and cash is essential to keep up with the rising medical bills for your daughter.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I fix the '97% blood' bug in my crime scene cleaner full guide?**

A: It is rarely a bug and usually a tiny splatter. Check the undersides of rugs, the legs of chairs you moved, and the very top of walls near the ceiling. Use the UV lamp in total darkness for the best results.

**Q: Can I complete the game without stealing anything?**

A: Technically yes, but your daughter's medical bills increase daily. Stealing valuables is the most effective way to stay ahead of the debt and unlock better equipment upgrades.

**Q: What happens if I get caught by the police?**

A: The game is mission-based; if you leave a scene without disposing of all bodies or evidence, you will fail the mission. You must ensure the "Evidence" and "Bodies" counters are at 100% before driving away in your truck.

**Q: How do I unlock the 'Smooth Operator' achievement?**

A: You must complete a full mission without breaking a single object (like vases or glass bottles) and without creating any new stains by tracking blood or tipping your bucket. It is easiest to do on the first apartment mission.
