## Advanced Cleaning Mechanics

A core part of any **crime scene cleaner achievements guide** is understanding how to maintain a 100% cleanup rate without creating new messes. Tracking blood is the most common reason players fail to hit 100%.

### Mop and Bucket Hygiene
*   **Check the Icon:** Look at the bottom-right of your screen. If the mop icon is red, you are just smearing blood.
*   **Change Water Often:** Once your bucket water turns dark red, every dip of the mop will actually make the floor dirtier. Use the nearest tap to refresh your water.
*   **Detergent Usage:** Only use detergent on porous surfaces like rugs, carpets, and curtains. Using it on hardwood is a waste of resources, and you have a limited supply early in the game.

### The "Smooth Operator" Achievement Trick
The **Smooth Operator** achievement requires you to complete a mission without breaking any items or creating new stains. This is notoriously difficult because the physics engine can be unpredictable. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** In the current 2026 version of the game, there is a known workaround. If you complete all tasks (100% blood, trash, furniture, evidence) and the game says "Ready to Leave," save and quit to the main menu. Completely close the game, restart it, and then finish the level by entering the truck. This often bypasses the "accidental breakage" check and awards the achievement.

## Furniture Replacement Strategy

To get 100% on furniture, you must return every moved object to its original "ghost" silhouette. If you can't find where a chair or cushion goes, hold the UV light button. The game will highlight the silhouettes of missing furniture in a faint blue or purple glow.

*   **Cushions:** These are the most commonly missed items. Check under tables and in corners.
*   **Paintings:** If you knocked a painting off the wall while cleaning, it must be re-hung.
*   **Body Disposal:** Ensure all bodies are placed in the truck and the tailgate is closed before you attempt to finish the level.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I go back and get missed achievements in Crime Scene Cleaner?**

A: Yes, you can replay any chapter from the main menu. Your upgrades and skill points carry over, making it much easier to achieve 100% on earlier levels like Bad Call.

**Q: Why is my blood cleanup stuck at 99%?**

A: This is usually due to "tracking." If you stepped in blood and walked across a clean floor, you left tiny footprints. Use the UV light and look specifically at the paths you walked between the blood pools and your bucket. Also, check the ceiling and the undersides of tables.

**Q: Do I need to steal everything for a 100% achievement score?**

A: While stealing isn't strictly required for the "100% Cleanup" achievement, there are separate achievements for stealing $50,000 total. Stealing also provides the cash needed for tool upgrades, which makes the **crime scene cleaner achievements guide** much easier to follow in later, more difficult levels.

**Q: How do I unlock the hidden basketball achievement?**

A: In Chapter 1, find the "Lucky Key" in the hidden apartment. Use it to access the alleyway basketball court. You only need to make one basket. If you're struggling, try jumping while throwing or crouching directly under the rim.
