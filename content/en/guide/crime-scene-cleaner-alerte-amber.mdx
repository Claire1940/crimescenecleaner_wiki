## Step-by-Step Cleanup Strategy

Follow these steps to ensure a professional finish in the **crime scene cleaner alerte amber** level. Efficiency is key to keeping your "Heat" low and your profit margins high.

### 1. Initial Assessment and Body Disposal
Before you start scrubbing, you must clear the floor of "trash"—in this case, the victims of Amber's rampage. There are several bodies scattered across the boxing ring and the private lounge areas. 

*   **The Boxing Ring:** Clear the fighter who was tortured in the center ring.
*   **The Bar Area:** Check behind the counter for the bartender who may have been poisoned.
*   **The Trafficking Ring:** Head to the back rooms to find the most gruesome evidence of Dave’s side business.

> **Warning:** Attempting to move heavy objects like sofas or industrial equipment while your trash bag is full will result in a "Something is blocking the way" error. Always empty your bins before attempting large-scale "interior design" changes.

### 2. Blood Removal and Sanitization
The amount of blood in this mission can be overwhelming, often making the player "see red." Use your industrial mop for floor surfaces and the power washer for the concrete areas in the back.

### 3. Evidence Collection and Looting
While your primary job is cleaning, your secondary goal is profit. The **crime scene cleaner alerte amber** mission is filled with "Quick Bucks" and high-value items. Look for the "Golden Ticket" and the "Golden Pants" belonging to a famous rapper who met his end here.

## Managing the "Heat" Mechanic

In the **crime scene cleaner alerte amber** mission, "Heat" represents the level of police suspicion. Every piece of evidence left behind—be it a bloodstain, a stray shell casing, or an unemptied trash bag—increases your risk.

1.  **Work Undercover:** Use the janitor's disguise to move through public-facing areas without raising eyebrows.
2.  **Dispose of Narcotics:** Dave was keeping the party alive with "drugs and booze." Ensure all illicit substances are incinerated or removed.
3.  **The Hand of Justice:** Sometimes you need to "speak where the hand of justice couldn't." This means destroying evidence that links the crime to Amber while highlighting the crimes of the victims (the traffickers).

> **Expert Tip:** If you find a police officer's badge in the mob hangout, do not just throw it away. Placing it in specific "lost and found" locations can actually lower your Heat by suggesting the officer was never there.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the code for the safe in the Amber Alert mission?**

A: The code is typically found on a sticky note located in the trafficking ring area, specifically near the computer terminal where Dave managed his "inventory."

**Q: How do I get past the "Something is blocking the way" message?**

A: This usually happens when you are trying to move a large object (like a body or furniture) while your inventory or trash bag is full. Clear your hands and try again. In some cases, it refers to small debris like pizza boxes or bottles wedged under the object.

**Q: Can I fail the crime scene cleaner alerte amber mission?**

A: You cannot "fail" in the traditional sense unless your Heat reaches 100%, leading to an arrest. However, leaving behind blood or bodies will result in a lower grade and less payout at the end of the level.

**Q: What is the significance of the Ninja Turtle reference?**

A: It is a fun Easter egg for players. The sewer area contains pizza boxes and green ooze-like stains, and the protagonist provides a short monologue about his childhood dreams of being a "fearless warrior" living in the sewers.
