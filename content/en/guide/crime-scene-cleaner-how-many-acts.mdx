## Act 1: The Foundation of Crime

Act 1 introduces you to the world of Big Jim and the various factions vying for power. These missions range from apartment stabbings to high-end spa massacres. Each level in Act 1 is designed to teach you a new mechanic, such as disposing of bodies in creative ways or using specialized tools like the ozone machine to remove persistent odors.

### Key Missions in Act 1

1.  **Bad Connection:** A "simple" drug deal gone wrong in a cramped apartment. This serves as the tutorial for blood cleaning and trash disposal.
2.  **Trial by Blood:** Set in a luxury spa. This mission introduces larger environmental puzzles and the need for keycards to access high-security areas.
3.  **Toxic Love:** A tragic scene in a downtown loft involving human trafficking and a father's revenge.
4.  **Short Circuit:** A grim "haunted" house scenario where a hacker's greed leads to a family tragedy.
5.  **Italian Job:** A massive shootout at a pizzeria. This is the first level where the body count hits double digits, requiring significant logistical planning.

> **💡 Tip:** In Act 1, always check the "Evidence" tab in your tablet. Missing a single piece of incriminating evidence can lead to a lower mission rating and less pay for your upgrades.

## Gameplay Mechanics and Progression

Regardless of which act you are in, the core gameplay loop remains consistent but evolves in complexity. You are tasked with three primary objectives:
1.  **Clean the Blood:** Using mops, sponges, and power washers.
2.  **Dispose of Evidence:** Bagging weapons, shell casings, and incriminating documents.
3.  **Remove Bodies:** Carrying them back to your truck or using environmental disposals (like pizza ovens or incinerators).

### Equipment Upgrades

As you progress through the acts, you will earn money to upgrade your gear. In 2026, the meta for efficient cleaning involves prioritizing the **Water Capacity** and **Suction Power** of your vacuum.

1.  **The Mop:** Your primary tool for large spills. Upgrading the reach allows you to clean ceilings without a ladder.
2.  **The Power Washer:** Essential for Act 2's outdoor sections. It consumes a lot of water but clears blood faster than any other tool.
3.  **Ozone Machine:** Used at the end of a mission to clear the "death smell." Ensure all bodies are gone before activating, or it won't work.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not leave your bucket in the middle of a hallway. If you knock it over, you will create a massive new mess that can double your cleaning time in the larger Act 2 levels.

## Secrets and Easter Eggs

The developers have hidden several secrets throughout the acts to reward exploration. One of the most famous is the **Sewer Secret** found in Act 2. By exploring the drainage systems in one of the mansion levels, players can unlock a special skin for their bucket that resembles a Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle.

These secrets often require finding hidden keys or solving environmental puzzles that aren't strictly necessary for the cleanup but add to the 100% completion score.
