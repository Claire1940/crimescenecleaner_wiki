### Why "Modern Art" Takes So Long
The "Modern Art" mission is notorious in the community for significantly inflating the **crime scene cleaner time to beat**. This is due to the "Floating Furniture" room and the dinosaur exhibit. You must find specific bones (like the tail bone and toe bone) and place them precisely to trigger the 100% furniture objective. Additionally, the maze area contains a TV puzzle that requires specific color-coding to unlock a secret room.

## Factors That Influence Playtime

Several variables can either shave hours off your total time or add frustrating delays to your progress.

### 1. Skill Tree Upgrades
Investing your skill points wisely is the best way to reduce the **crime scene cleaner time to beat**. 
*   **Cleaning Scent:** Highlighting stains is mandatory for finding that last 1% of blood hidden under a rug or behind a door.
*   **No Footprints:** This is a late-game game-changer. It prevents you from tracking blood back onto clean floors, which often forces players to re-clean areas they've already finished.
*   **Bucket Capacity:** Reducing the number of trips to the water source saves significant time on large maps like the Warehouse.

### 2. Secret Hunting
Every mission contains hidden secrets and cassette tapes. Finding these often requires "Parkour"—using ladders, jumping across pipes, or breaking through boarded-up windows. If you are following a completionist path, expect to add 15–20 minutes per level just for exploration.

### 3. The "99% Blood" Glitch
One of the most common time-wasters is the hunt for the final speck of blood. In missions like "Short Circuit" (the Spa), blood can sometimes clip into the geometry or hide on the ceiling. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always check the "Cleaner Scent" view before moving furniture back. If you place a heavy table over a tiny stain, the game may still count it as dirty, but you won't be able to see the stain through the object.

## Tools of the Trade: Efficiency Comparison

Using the wrong tool for the job can double your mission time. While the mop is your bread and butter, knowing when to switch to the sponge or pressure washer is vital.

| Tool | Best Used For... | Time Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Mop** | Large floor areas and basic walls. | **Fast** for coverage, but leaves footprints. |
| **Sponge** | Fragile items (vases, paintings) and precision cleaning. | **Slow**, but prevents accidental item breakage. |
| **Pressure Washer** | Pools, outdoor areas, and heavy industrial grime. | **Very Fast** for outdoor, but requires refills. |
| **UV Lamp** | Dark corners and basement areas. | **Essential** for finding hidden stains. |
| **Ozone Machine** | Finalizing the "clean" status of a room. | **Set-and-forget** tool used at the end. |

## Path to 100% Completion (All Achievements)

To truly "beat" the game in 2026, you must secure all 50 achievements. Some of these are hidden or require specific actions that aren't related to cleaning.

*   **"Bad Call" Achievement:** Complete the mission without breaking a single item. This requires using the sponge almost exclusively and moving very slowly.
*   **"Long Night" Achievement:** Ignore Big Jim's call and wait out the timer. Note: This results in a "Game Over," but grants the achievement.
*   **"Basketball Pro":** Successfully make a basket in the museum level.
*   **"Pyramid Destroyer":** Collapse the pyramid of cans/bottles found in specific levels.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I save my progress mid-mission?**

A: Yes, the game allows you to save mid-mission. This is highly recommended for the longer levels like the Museum or Warehouse, which can take upwards of two hours to 100%.

**Q: Does the difficulty setting change the crime scene cleaner time to beat?**

A: While there aren't traditional "Easy/Hard" sliders, the complexity of the levels increases as you progress. Later levels have more bodies to dispose of, more evidence to collect, and more intricate furniture layouts to restore.

**Q: What happens if I leave a mission without 100% cleaning?**

A: You can finish a mission as soon as you meet the "Minimum Requirements" (usually around 60-70% clean). This will drastically reduce your **crime scene cleaner time to beat**, but you will earn less money and fewer skill points, making later levels much harder.

**Q: Are there any missable achievements?**

A: Most achievements can be earned by replaying missions via the level select screen. However, achievements like "Smooth Operator" (completing a mission without breaking anything) are much easier to do on the very first level.
