## Ground Floor Walkthrough: Kitchen and Living Area

The mission begins in a stunning apartment. Your first stop should be the bathroom on the left to flush the toilet—a small task that contributes to your overall completion. Once that is done, focus on the kitchen and dining area.

### Cleaning the Kitchen and Dining Room
The kitchen is relatively clean compared to the rest of the unit, but it holds vital evidence. Use your garbage can to clear out the trash first. When mopping the rug in the dining area, remember that plain water won't suffice; you must use a strong detergent to lift the stains.

1.  **Interact with the second drawer** in the kitchen to find hidden cash.
2.  **Collect Evidence #1:** The kitchen phone on the counter.
3.  **The Chandelier Body:** There is a body caught high up. Instead of climbing, grab a nearby wine bottle and throw it at the body to knock it down. This prevents the body from dripping fresh blood onto your clean floors while you work.

### The Secret Room and Living Area
Behind the large painting in the living area, you will find a hidden room containing a body and a significant amount of blood. 

| Item Type | Location | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Cassette Tape #1** | Secret Room | Found on the right side of the room. |

> **Warning:** Be careful when mopping near the vases. If you hit them with a mop, they will shatter, which can negatively impact your final score if you are aiming for a "no breakage" run. Use a sponge for precision cleaning around delicate objects.

## First Floor Walkthrough: The Bedroom and Bath

The second level of the apartment in **crime scene cleaner act 3** is where the most intense cleaning takes place. You will need your UV lamp to spot hidden biological traces that aren't visible to the naked eye.

### Handling the Glass Balcony
A common mistake players make in this act is trying to use the pressure washer on the *outside* of the glass balcony. The blood is actually on the *inside*. Save your time and water by focusing your efforts on the interior side of the glass panels.

## Unlocking Secret Achievements

There are two specific achievements you should aim for during your run of **crime scene cleaner act 3**. Both require interacting with the environment in specific ways that aren't immediately obvious.

### 1. The "Get Lucky" Achievement
To unlock this, you must crack the safe located behind a painting on the ground floor. 
*   First, you must reset the fuse box found in the secret room.
*   Once the power is restored, interact with the painting to reveal the safe.
*   Complete the hacking mini-game by timing your button presses with the scrolling numbers.
*   Inside, you'll find over $7,000 in cash and the achievement will pop.

### 2. The "Electric Field" Achievement
This is a miscellaneous achievement that can be completed in the upstairs bathroom.
*   Go to the kitchen on the ground floor and pick up the **toaster**.
*   Carry the toaster all the way to the upstairs bathroom.
*   Drop the toaster into the bathtub (which should be filled with water/blood).
*   This will trigger the "Electric Field" achievement.

## Final Steps for a 100% Deception Score

Before you pack up your truck and leave, you must ensure the "Deception" criteria are met. This means no trace of the crime can remain, and the apartment must look lived-in again.

1.  **Furniture Reset:** Check your HUD counter. Every chair, lamp, and cushion moved during the struggle must be returned to its original silhouette. In Act 3, pay close attention to the drum symbols and the cushions on the sofa.
2.  **The Laundry Room:** Before exiting, check the small laundry room near the stairs. It contains a golden face statue and several stacks of cash that are easy to miss.
3.  **Body Disposal:** Ensure all bodies are bagged and placed in your truck's bin. 
4.  **Trash Removal:** Double-check the kitchen and the upstairs balcony for small pieces of trash (highlighted in green with your senses).

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I reach 100% blood removal in crime scene cleaner act 3?**

A: Check the interior of the glass balcony on the first floor and the area under the stairs. Small droplets often hide behind furniture silhouettes. Also, ensure you have cleaned the inside of the bathtub thoroughly.

**Q: Where is the fuse box located in Toxic Love?**

A: The fuse box is located inside the secret room on the ground floor, which is hidden behind a large painting in the living area. You must reset these fuses to open the locked electronic door upstairs.

**Q: Do I need to put the toaster back to get a 100% score?**

A: While moving the toaster is required for the "Electric Field" achievement, you do not strictly need to return it to the kitchen to achieve a 100% deception score, though it is good practice to avoid leaving "out of place" objects.

**Q: How do I find the second cassette tape?**

A: The first is in the ground floor secret room. The second is in the upstairs bathroom, tucked away near the bathtub area where the final piece of evidence is found.
