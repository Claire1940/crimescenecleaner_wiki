## Bad Call & Trial By Blood: The Nightmare Begins

The first two levels set the tone for the Nightmare experience. In **Bad Call**, your priority is locating the Polaroid camera immediately, as it is required to unlock the final room in the attic.

### Bad Call Checklist
| Objective | Location Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Total Bodies** | 13 (Check Secret Spot x2 and Hidden Apartment) |
| **Evidence Pieces** | 6 (Dream Catchers + Polaroid Camera) |
| **Key Item** | **Polaroid Camera** found in the attic hatch |
| **Secret Room** | Attic Backroom (Unlocked via 4 Polaroid photos) |

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Before scrubbing any blood in Bad Call, head left from the truck, climb the ladder, and enter the attic via the dripping hatch to grab the camera. This saves significant backtracking later.

In **Trial By Blood**, the challenge shifts to a scavenger hunt for locker keycards. You must uncover eight different cards to access all bodies and evidence hidden within the spa's locker rooms.

### Trial By Blood Locker Guide
*   **M005:** Found near the Pharaoh Statue.
*   **M014:** Located inside the Maintenance Room.
*   **M017:** Found on the side of the Pool.
*   **M025:** Hidden under the body in the VIP Room.
*   **M034:** Hanging on a lamp by the sauna.
*   **W018:** Found inside locker M034.
*   **W024:** Located in the Solarium.
*   **W042:** Hidden in a sink in the stone room.

## Toxic Love & Short Circuit: Labyrinths and Electronics

**Toxic Love** introduces a mannequin-filled nightmare. The "Labyrinth" section is particularly treacherous, requiring you to break planks and find hidden buttons. In the Computer Room, look for a hidden button to the left of the desk to reveal the music disk.

**Short Circuit** features a supernatural twist with floating furniture and mid-air bodies. You will need to collect 13 pieces of evidence, the highest in the game, many of which are Smart Speakers scattered throughout the house.

| Level | Key Evidence | Secret Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Toxic Love** | Razor blades in the bath | Hidden room behind the Computer Room desk |
| **Short Circuit** | 13 Smart Speakers | Floating rock outside the Office window |

## Italian Job & Affair With Death: Puzzles and Codes

The **Italian Job** nightmare map features a terrifying animatronic duck that stalks the restaurant. You must navigate a bricked-up office and a "Modern Art Museum" hidden behind the old woman's apartment. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** In the Restaurant, the door will slam behind you when you enter the bathroom. Look for the key inside the pizza boxes in the left cubicle to escape.

**Affair With Death** is the most code-heavy level in the game. Success depends on your ability to solve environmental riddles involving ducks, paintings, and TV symbols.

## Friendly Fire & Party's Over: Rituals and Reality

**Friendly Fire** requires a massive cleanup of Tyler’s apartment, but the real challenge is collecting **100 cardboard boxes** to recreate a cat’s dream centerpiece. You must also find a missing bell to summon the "Taxi Fish," which transports you to a black-and-white dimension containing the music disk.

In **Party's Over**, you are tasked with cleaning a cultist retreat. This level culminates in an "Ancient Temple" ritual. You must find three orbs and place them on pedestals to release a body trapped under a grate.

### Party's Over Ritual Items
1.  **Candlesticks:** Collected from pedestals outside Cabins A, B, and C.
2.  **Skull:** Found in a kitchen cupboard inside the main house.
3.  **Metal Barrels:** 5 total, must be placed against the back wall of the lounge.

## Modern Art & Call In Dead: The Final Cleanup

The **Modern Art** level is a logistical test. It features a "Cloud Level" with eight colored portals. The difficulty lies in "Returning Items," where you must move dozens of sculptures, paintings, and furniture pieces between different exhibition rooms.

### Modern Art Portal Directory
*   **Black:** Foyer
*   **White:** Furniture Exhibition
*   **Black/White:** Cafeteria
*   **Red:** Duck Room
*   **Grey:** Music Exhibition
*   **Blue:** Upper Passage
*   **Bronze:** Sewage Exhibition
*   **Green:** Ancient Exhibition

Finally, **Call In Dead** takes you to a massive industrial warehouse. You must manage six different "Halls," using generators to power conveyor belts that transport bodies and trash to a central disposal hole. 

> **💡 Pro Tip:** In Hall 02, climb the ladder behind the Arms truck to find a crawlspace. This leads to the power button that extends the upper platform, allowing you to reach the second generator.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock Nightmare Mode in Crime Scene Cleaner?**

A: Nightmare Mode typically unlocks after completing the main story campaign on standard difficulty. Once unlocked, you can access the Nightmare versions of each map from the level selection computer in Kovalsky's apartment.

**Q: Are there new tools available in the 2026 Nightmare update?**

A: While the core tools remain the same, the Nightmare update emphasizes the use of the Axe for clearing environmental hazards (like vines and planks) and the Polaroid Camera for solving specific level secrets.

**Q: What happens if I miss a single piece of evidence in a crime scene cleaner nightmare mode walkthrough?**

A: To achieve a "Perfect" rating, you must collect 100% of the evidence and bodies. If you miss one, you can use your "Cleaner Sense" (highlighting tool) to scan the environment. Evidence often glows with a distinct outline.

**Q: Can I play Nightmare Mode in co-op?**

A: As of the 2026 update, Nightmare Mode is designed as a solo challenge to emphasize the atmospheric horror and resource management, though some community mods may allow for multi-cleaner sessions.
