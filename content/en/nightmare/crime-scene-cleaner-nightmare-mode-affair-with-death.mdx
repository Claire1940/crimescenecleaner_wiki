## Mission Overview and Key Objectives

In the Nightmare version of "Affair with Death," the scale of the environment remains a Level 3, but the "twisted" nature of the map means many shortcuts from the standard game are blocked or require new keys. Your primary goal is to clean the massive blood spills, dispose of the single body that drops from the balcony, and return 30 pieces of furniture to their rightful places.

| Objective Type | Total Count | Primary Locations |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bodies to Dispose** | 1 | Drops from First Floor Bedroom to Pool |
| **Evidence Pieces** | 3 | Bedroom, Basement, Lounge |
| **Furniture to Move** | 30 | Hall, Kitchen, Lounge, Bedrooms |
| **Secret Collectibles** | 1 | Amber Music Disc (Pool Area) |

> **💡 Tip:** Always start by finding the water points. In Nightmare mode, the starting bathroom on the first floor is your initial source, but you will need to unlock the Driveway and Pool areas to access more convenient refill stations.

## The First Floor: Escaping the Bathroom

Your nightmare begins in the **First Floor Bathroom**. Unlike the standard start at the pool, you are locked in. You must find the door handle inside a black and green case located near the bathtub. Attach the handle to the door to step out into the hallway.

### The Duck Puzzle and Stair Gate
Once in the hallway, you will encounter a gate locked with a four-digit code. To solve this, head into the **First Upstairs Bedroom**. You will notice colored baskets (White, Red, Blue, Green) and ducks scattered everywhere. You must count the ducks of each color to form the code.

| Duck Color | Count / Digit | Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **White** | 7 | Found on shelves, floors, and inside the safe |
| **Red** | 5 | Found on the desk, bedframe, and safe |
| **Blue** | 3 | Found between beanbags and by the guitar |
| **Green** | 4 | Found on the desk and by the TV cabinet |

**The Duck Code is 7534.** Use this to open the first gate. However, a second gate further down the stairs requires a different code: **2501**. These digits are found by looking at yellow dashes on walls in the Restricted Area and the Driveway.

## Ground Floor: The Surveillance Room and Garage

After descending the stairs, you will find the main hall barricaded. You must enter the **Surveillance Room** to progress. This room contains a TV displaying a **Green Diamond** symbol—take note of this for the final puzzle.

### The Egyptian Puzzle
In a small room behind the surveillance desk, you will find three statues: Nefertiti, Horus, and a Sitting Pharaoh. This is a math puzzle where you must determine the value of each figure:
*   **Nefertiti's Head:** 3
*   **Horus:** 4
*   **Sitting Pharaoh:** 12

Input the code **1234** into the keypad. The wall will rotate to reveal **Puzzle Key 03**, which unlocks the glass doors leading to the Driveway.

### The Garage Spanners
Head out to the Driveway and enter the partially open garage. On the wall, spanners are arranged to form numbers. By interacting with them, you can decipher the code **7048**. Use this to unlock the tool room inside the garage to retrieve one of the three **Elephant Statuettes** needed for the basement access.

## The Basement and Kitchen Challenges

To enter the basement, you must place three Elephant Statuettes on the plinths in the main stairwell. The order must be **White, Red, then Black**. This opens a hidden passage.

### Basement Keypad and Evidence
The basement contains the second piece of evidence (a Dreamcatcher) and a gated area. To find the code, look at the painting next to the gate which depicts a Dart, a Billiard Ball, Money, and a Knife.
1.  **Dart:** Look at the dartboard through the bars to see the number **9**.
2.  **Billiard Ball:** Identify the missing ball number from the set: **4**.
3.  **Money:** Pick up all the stacks of money on the trolley to reveal the number **7**.
4.  **Knife:** Inspect the "Knife My Wife" magazine and rotate it to find **5**.

**The Basement Code is 9475.** Entering this area allows you to retrieve the Kitchen door handle.

### The Kitchen Safe and Painting Puzzle
Once in the Kitchen, count the bottles on the counter to open the safe under the bar. The bottle count provides the code **9546**. Inside is a key for the display cabinet. 

You must then find three paintings with holes and place them on the numbered wall in the kitchen. The orientation of the holes reveals the code for the Lounge: **1408**.

| Painting Location | Hole Position | Resulting Digit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hall Corridor** | Top Left | 1 |
| **Near Pottery** | Two Holes | 4 |
| **Display Cabinet** | Bottom Center | 8 |

## The Lounge and the TV Symbol Puzzle

The Lounge is the final interior hurdle in the **crime scene cleaner nightmare mode affair with death** mission. The room contains six TVs that must display specific colored symbols to unlock the exit to the pool. Throughout the level, you should have noted these symbols on various TV screens.

**The Correct TV Sequence:**
1.  **Orange Star** (Found in First Floor Bedroom)
2.  **Pink Heart** (Found in Second Bedroom)
3.  **Green Diamond** (Found in Surveillance Room)
4.  **White Cross** (Found in Garage)
5.  **Blue Circle** (Found in Basement)
6.  **Pink Triangle** (Found in the Secret Laundry Room behind the "Rome Empire" book)

> **⚠️ Warning:** Interacting with the "Rome Empire" book on the shelf opens a secret room. If you enter and press the red button, the door may close, so ensure you have finished cleaning the Lounge before exploring the secret compartment.

## The Pool Area: Amber Disc Collectible

The final area is the Pool. This is where the body from the first-floor balcony finally lands. After cleaning the impact site, you must hunt for the **Amber Music Disc**.

To get the disc, you need to collect **16 Puzzle Cubes** scattered around the pool and the basketball court. 
*   Two cubes are submerged in the pool; use the power box at the far end to drain the water.
*   Other cubes are hidden on high ledges near the plants and behind the basketball hoop.
*   One cube is tucked away in a corner of the secret area that was used for the "Secret Spot" in standard mode.

Once you have all 16, arrange them on the wall in the basketball area to form a yellow duck. A hidden door will open, revealing the disc. For more details on the game's mechanics, visit the official [Crime Scene Cleaner Steam Page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1040200/Crime_Scene_Cleaner/).

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the body in the Affair with Death Nightmare mode?**

A: There is only one body. It is initially located in the First Floor Bedroom. When you open the balcony doors, the body falls to the Pool area below. You must go downstairs to collect it and bring it to the truck.

**Q: I can't find the third Elephant Statuette. Where is it?**

A: The three statues are located: 1) Under the stairs, 2) In the Surveillance Room, and 3) Inside the locked tool room in the Garage (requires the spanner code 7048).

**Q: How do I open the safe in the duck bedroom?**

A: Interact with the safe to hear a sequence of sounds. Click the duck buttons on the wall to mimic the melody. This will unlock the safe, allowing you to count the final ducks needed for the gate code.

**Q: What is the code for the final Lounge door in Crime Scene Cleaner Nightmare Mode Affair with Death?**

A: The code is 1408. It is derived from placing the three "hole" paintings on the numbered wall in the kitchen and reading the digits visible through the cutouts.
