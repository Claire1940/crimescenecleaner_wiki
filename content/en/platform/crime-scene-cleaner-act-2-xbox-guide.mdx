## Step-by-Step Guide to Key Cards and Lockers

The progression in Act 2 is heavily gated by key cards found inside lockers. This "breadcrumb trail" can be confusing if you lose track of which locker belongs to which room. Follow the sequence below to ensure you don't miss any critical items.

### The Initial Hack and Cassette #1
Upon entering the facility, head toward the male changing rooms. You will encounter a locked door with a keypad. This is your first interaction with the hacking mechanic on Xbox. A line will move across a sequence of numbers; you must press **A** exactly when the line overlaps the number. 

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If you miss too many times, your "health" for the hack will deplete. Take your time and focus on the rhythm rather than rushing the sequence.

Once inside this office/security room, look at the desk immediately. You will find **Cassette Tape #1** resting there. This room also contains important switches for the facility's utility systems.

### Draining the Pool
The central feature of this level is the large pool filled with blood-tainted water. You cannot clean the floor of the pool until it is empty. Locate the drainage switch (found in the office area you just hacked) and activate it. 

Once the water level drops, jump into the empty pool. In the center of the floor, you will find the **M7 Locker Key Card**. This card is the "key" to the next stage of the level's item hunt.

## Finding All Cassettes and Secrets

Finding all the collectibles is a core part of this **crime scene cleaner act 2 xbox guide**. These items are often tucked away in corners that you might overlook while focused on mopping up blood.

### Cassette #2: The M7 Locker
After retrieving the M7 card from the pool, head back to the male changing rooms. Locate locker M7 and open it. **Cassette Tape #2** is stored inside. Collecting this early is recommended so you don't have to return to this wing later.

### Secret #1 and Cassette #3: The VIP Room
To access the first secret area, you must complete the locker chain:
1.  Go to the **Sun Tanning Room** to find the Women's 24 key card.
2.  Open locker **W24** in the women's changing room to find the M34 key card.
3.  Open locker **M34** in the male changing room to find the **VIP Key Card**.
4.  Return to the main office desk and look for a button or card reader labeled **VIP**.

Activating this will open a hidden door. Enter the VIP lounge to trigger the "Secret Found" notification. Near the lion statue in this opulent room, you will find **Cassette Tape #3**.

### Secret #2: The Hidden Poster
While in the VIP area, ensure you pick up the **Women's 018 key card**. Head back to the women's changing rooms and locate locker 018. When you open it, look closely at the poster on the back wall of the locker. Interacting with or observing this area thoroughly will trigger the second secret of the level.

## Cleaning Strategies for Xbox Players

Cleaning in Act 2 is more labor-intensive than Act 1. The pool area is vast, and blood splatters often reach the high ceilings and glass partitions. 

*   **Use the Power Washer:** For the pool itself, the power washer is much faster than the mop. Start from the top edges and work your way down to the drain.
*   **Sensor Usage:** Frequently use your "Cleaner Sense" (typically mapped to the shoulder buttons or triggers) to highlight small blood spots. In the spa, blood often hides under the benches and behind the tanning beds.
*   **Waste Management:** There are many "body parts" and pieces of evidence in this level. Ensure you have your bin nearby to avoid walking back and forth across the large gym floor.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when moving buckets of water near the pool. If you spill a dirty bucket into a partially cleaned area, you will create a massive mess that significantly adds to your completion time.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I hack the keypad on Xbox?**

A: The hacking minigame requires you to wait for a moving horizontal line to pass over a highlighted number. Press the **A button** at the exact moment of overlap. Successfully hitting all numbers opens the door, while misses reduce your hacking health bar.

**Q: I can't find the last 1% of blood in the pool room. Where is it?**

A: Check the high glass panels surrounding the pool and the underside of the diving boards. Also, check the drains; sometimes blood "pools" inside the geometry of the drain grate. Using this **crime scene cleaner act 2 xbox guide** alongside your in-game cleaner vision should highlight these stubborn spots.

**Q: What do the cassette tapes do?**

A: Cassette tapes are collectibles that unlock new music tracks. You can listen to these tracks while cleaning or back at your home base (the van). They do not provide gameplay buffs but are required for completionist achievements.

**Q: Can I redo the level if I missed a secret?**

A: Yes, you can replay any completed act from the level selection menu in your van. Any cassettes you have already found will remain collected, allowing you to focus specifically on the secrets or cleaning tasks you missed.
