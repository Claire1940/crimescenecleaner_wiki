## Accessing the New Content and Act Selection

The 2026 update has overhauled how players interact with the mission map. Previously, missions were a linear progression, but the introduction of "Act 1" and the "Community Area" (effectively the Act 2 expansion) allows for a more modular experience. To access these new challenges on your PS5, you must first ensure you have cleared the initial story requirements in Act 1.

Once you are in the garage hub, interacting with the mission truck will now present a toggle between different chapters. If you find the Community Area missions locked, you must head to the cellar and check your computer.

### Unlocking Missions via Email
Unlike the early story missions that trigger automatically, the Act 2/Community missions require manual activation.
1.  **Head to the Cellar:** Go down the stairs from the main garage.
2.  **Access the PC:** Log into your workstation.
3.  **Read Emails:** Look for messages regarding the "Secret Lab" and "Game Development Hell."
4.  **Accept the Contract:** Once accepted, the red mission marker will appear on your truck’s map.

## The Secret Equipment Room in Kovalsky's Apartment

One of the most significant additions in the recent update is the professional tool room. This isn't just a cosmetic upgrade; it serves as your central hub for managing skins, music, and the skill tree. Many players overlook this room because it is cleverly tucked away within the existing apartment layout.

### How to Find the Hidden Closet
To find the new room, head to the cellar where your computer is located. Look for a large, unassuming closet near the back wall. Interacting with the doors will reveal a hidden walk-in workshop. Inside, you will find:

*   **Tool Display:** Every tool you have unlocked is displayed here. You can interact with them to change their appearance if you have unlocked new skins.
*   **The Skill Tree:** A physical representation of your progress where you can spend points on cleaning efficiency and stealth.
*   **Music Station:** Here you can play the seven new music tracks added in the latest update and manage the cassettes you've collected during jobs.
*   **Cassette Collection:** A shelf dedicated to the audio logs and music tapes found hidden in various maps.

## Mission Guide: The Secret Lab

The Secret Lab is arguably the most complex map in the Act 2 content. With a body count of 13 and a massive layout involving high-tech machinery and narrow corridors, your cleaning strategy must be methodical.

### Step 1: Body Disposal
With 13 bodies to move, the "Body Bag" upgrade is essential. On the PS5, use the haptic triggers to feel the weight of the objects; this helps in determining if you are about to collide with a wall and leave a new blood trail. Dispose of the bodies in the designated "incinerator" or "acid vat" areas found within the lab to clear space for scrubbing.

### Step 2: High-Tech Cleaning
The lab features sensitive electronics. Using the power washer indiscriminately will cause electrical shorts, potentially failing secondary objectives.
*   **Mop for Floors:** Use the mop for the bulk of the floor cleaning.
*   **Sponge for Consoles:** Use the sponge for delicate cleaning around the computer terminals.
*   **UV Light:** Essential for finding "invisible" chemical spills that don't show up as standard blood.

### Step 3: Evidence Collection
There are several pieces of incriminating evidence (hard drives and folders) scattered across the lab. Ensure you collect these before you start the final "wash down," as they can be easily obscured by soap suds or moved by the power washer's spray.

| Tool | Best Use in Secret Lab | Priority Upgrade |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Power Washer** | Large concrete vats and walls | Pressure Nozzle |
| **Mop** | Main hallways and tile floors | Absorbency |
| **Ozone Machine** | Removing chemical odors | Radius |
| **UV Lamp** | Detecting hidden spills | Battery Life |

## PS5 Specific Features and Optimization

Playing *Crime Scene Cleaner* on PS5 offers several advantages, provided you have your settings optimized. The DualSense controller provides tactile feedback when you are scrubbing a particularly stubborn stain, vibrating more intensely as the surface becomes clean.

### Recommended Settings for PS5
*   **Performance Mode:** Highly recommended for the Secret Lab mission to maintain 60 FPS during heavy particle effects (like steam or blood spray).
*   **Adaptive Triggers:** Set to "Medium." This provides resistance when using the power washer, making the experience feel more grounded.
*   **Field of View (FOV):** Increase to 90. This helps you spot evidence in the periphery of your vision while cleaning tight corners.

> **Warning:** Be careful when using the "Throw" mechanic on PS5. In the new Act 2 maps, many environments have breakable glass. If you throw a bucket or a tool and break a window, you may create more work for yourself by having to clean up glass shards.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Act 2 missions in Crime Scene Cleaner?**

A: You must complete the primary story missions in Act 1. Once finished, go to the cellar in your apartment, check your computer for new emails, and accept the contracts for the "Secret Lab" or "Game Development Hell." These will then appear on your mission map under the "Community" tab.

**Q: Where is the new tool room located?**

A: The new room is in the cellar of Kovalsky's apartment. Look for a closet door that you can interact with. Opening it reveals a hidden workshop where you can change skins, view your skill tree, and play collected music.

**Q: What is the best way to handle 13 bodies in the Secret Lab?**

A: Use the body bag upgrade to prevent leaving new blood trails. Move all bodies to a central "disposal zone" (like the acid vats) before you begin deep cleaning the floors. This prevents you from accidentally stepping in blood and tracking it back over cleaned areas.

**Q: Are there any PS5-exclusive features in this guide?**

A: This **crime scene cleaner act 2 ps5 guide** highlights the use of DualSense haptic feedback for cleaning intensity and the importance of Performance Mode for maintaining high frame rates in the new, more complex maps like the Secret Lab.

**Q: Can I redo missions in Act 2?**

A: Yes, once a mission is unlocked and completed, you can select it at any time from the mission truck by switching between the "Act 1" and "Community" tabs on the selection screen.
